Job Description

About Ola: The Ola Group is a dynamic conglomerate comprising ANI Technologies, Ola Electric, and Krutrim, each contributing to our mission of redefining mobility, advancing sustainable transportation, and pioneering AI innovation. At ANI Technologies, we operate Ola Cabs, India's leading ride-hailing platform, serving over 250 million customers across 3 continents, offering employees a fast-paced, tech-driven environment where innovation thrives. Ola Electric is at the forefront of the electric vehicle revolution, with our state-of-the-art Futurefactory in Tamil Nadu, producing over 5 million electric two-wheelers annually and creating thousands of jobs. Krutrim, our AI arm, is dedicated to developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence models and infrastructure, empowering India to lead in the digital age. Job Summary: As a SDE1, you will design, build, and optimize mission-critical software that powers Ola’s large-scale operations. You will work across application development, platform integration, and system performance solving real engineering challenges in a fast-paced, innovation-driven environment. Key Result Areas: Design, develop, and deploy scalable business applications and internal platforms. Build and maintain integrations across services, APIs, and internal systems to drive operational efficiency. Work with cross-functional teams to translate business requirements into robust technical solutions. Perform debugging, performance tuning, and end-to-end code optimization. Contribute to automation, workflow enhancements, and engineering best practices. Competency Requirements: B.Tech/M.Tech in Computer Science, IT, Software Engineering, or related fields. Strong understanding of data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented programming. Proficiency in Java, Python, or similar backend languages. Familiarity with databases, REST APIs, microservices, and Git. Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills. Ability to thrive in a dynamic, collaborative engineering culture. Perks of working with Ola: Build technology that impacts millions at massive scale. Work with top engineers solving complex, high-performance problems. Exposure to cutting-edge systems across mobility, EV, and AI businesses. High ownership and visibility with leadership. Fast-track career growth in a high-velocity engineering environment.
