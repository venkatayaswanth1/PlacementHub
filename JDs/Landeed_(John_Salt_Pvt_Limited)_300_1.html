Job Description

Landeed is a Y Combinator (S22) alumni company, backed by top-tier investors, that is solving India's massive property dispute problem by offering the nation's fastest and most comprehensive property title search engine. Their platform retrieves critical legal documents in seconds, effectively building the operating system for property ownership in India and transforming a $200 billion industry currently responsible for two-thirds of all court cases. Having recently raised $5 million in Series X funding, Landeed is scaling rapidly, with over 2 million users across 24+ Indian states. The role of an early-stage Software Engineer involves working directly with the founding team to design scalable backend systems, build AI/ML solutions for data extraction and analytics, and create intuitive frontends, requiring strong skills in languages like Python and JavaScript. This is a high-impact, wealth-building opportunity to join the first 50 employees of a fast-growing startup, gain expertise in proptech and legal tech, and directly influence product strategy that impacts millions of Indian families.
