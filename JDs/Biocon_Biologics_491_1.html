Job Description

Job Description – Analytical Sciences Position: Scientist for Physicochemical Group (ADL and CDL) Analytical Development Lab & Characterisation development Lab Role Summary We are looking for enthusiastic and motivated individuals to join our Analytical Sciences team. As a campus hire, you will be trained and guided to contribute to the development, qualification and transfer of analytical methods used in biopharmaceutical development. Key Responsibilities: • Assist in conducting experiments for analytical method development, qualification, and transfer using techniques such as chromatography, electrophoresis, UV/Vis spectrophotometry, and mass spectrometry. • Learn and apply advanced analytical tools and spectroscopic methods to study molecular structures and properties. • Accurately document experimental procedures, observations, and results in electronic laboratory notebooks, following good documentation practices. • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to share data, compile reports, and participate in team meetings. • Support data analysis and trending to ensure consistency and reliability of results. • Help prepare scientific documents including method development reports, protocols, and standard operating procedures. • Conduct literature reviews to support experimental design and troubleshooting. • Assist in maintaining laboratory instruments and equipment and support installation and documentation of new instruments. • Help manage laboratory inventory including reagents, reference standards, consumables, and samples. • Participate in equipment scheduling and resource optimization for efficient lab operations. • Work effectively in a team environment and contribute to a collaborative and productive workplace. Key Skillset • Master’s degree (MTech) in chemistry, Biochemistry, Biotechnology, Pharmaceutical Sciences, or related field. • Basic understanding of analytical techniques such as chromatography, spectroscopy, and electrophoresis, along with general laboratory practices. • Strong attention to detail and eagerness to learn new methods, tools, and technologies in a fast-paced research environment. • Good communication and teamwork skills
