Job Description

We are seeking a motivated Associate to support our Launch Business Development activities. This entry-to-mid level position provides an excellent opportunity to gain experience in the space industry while contributing to business growth initiatives. The ideal candidate will be detail-oriented, analytical, and passionate about space technology and commercial launch services. Key Responsibilities Market Research: Conduct detailed market research on potential customers, competitors, and industry trends in the launch services sector. Lead Generation: Identify and qualify potential customers and business opportunities through various research methods and databases. Proposal Support: Assist in preparation of proposals, presentations, and marketing materials for potential customers and partners. Customer Relationship Management: Support customer engagement activities, maintain CRM systems, and track interaction history. Data Analysis: Analyze market data, customer requirements, and competitive intelligence to support strategic decision-making. Administrative Supportrovide administrative support for business development activities including scheduling, documentation, and follow-up activities. Industry Monitoring: Track industry developments, regulatory changes, and market opportunities relevant to launch services. Qualifications Education: Final Year Engineering Graduate only 2026 Technical Skills: Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, CRM systems, and data analysis tools. Research Skills: Strong research and analytical capabilities with ability to synthesize complex information. Communication Skills: Good written and verbal communication skills with attention to detail. Desired Attributes Eagerness to learn about the space industry and launch services market. Proactive and self-motivated with ability to work independently and as part of a team. Strong organizational skills and ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously. People who have been in organising committees of the college festivals or part of student senate with an eagerness to improve the business understanding and develop into techno-commercial leaders are preferred.
