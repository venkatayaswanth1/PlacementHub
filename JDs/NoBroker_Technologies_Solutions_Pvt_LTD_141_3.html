Job Description

About Us: NoBroker.com is the world's largest brokerage-free real estate marketplace, dedicated to transforming the real estate experience with cutting-edge products. Founded by IIT Bombay, IIT Kanpur, and IIM Ahmedabad alumni in 2014, we've raised $366M from prominent investors like General Atlantic, Tiger Global, and Saif Partners Our comprehensive real estate ecosystem includes NoBrokerHood for community management, NoBroker HomeServices for property management, and a range of services like Legal Services, Rental Agreements, Packers & Movers, HomeLoans, Rent Payments, and more. ConvoZen.AI: We are also proud to offer ConvoZen.AI, our state-of-the-art conversational AI tool. ConvoZen.AI leverages advanced NLP and large language model (LLM) technologies to provide organizations with actionable insights from customer conversations. This innovative solution is revolutionizing how businesses interact with their customers, making it a focal point of our data science endeavors. Role: The Applied Scientist at NoBroker.com (and ConvoZen.AI) is expected to drive innovation through research and development of data science products that directly impact millions of users. This role is ideal for exceptionally smart individuals who combine strong theoretical foundations with hands-on skills to build production-ready systems and keep in touch with the latest technology trends. Successful candidates will be instrumental in defining the next generation of our AI-powered solutions. Responsibilities ● Conduct pioneering research into state-of-the-art algorithms, with a focus on Large Language Models (LLMs), Text-to-Speech (TTS), Speech-to-Text (STT), and Speech-to-Speech (STS) to solve complex challenges in AI. You will be responsible for staying ahead of the latest academic and industry advancements. ● Design, build, and deploy end-to-end, scalable AI-driven systems. This includes fine-tuning and optimizing LLMs and multi-modal models for real-time conversational AI, as well as developing novel applications based on your research. ● Analyze large, complex datasets to extract actionable insights and identify opportunities for automation and optimization. Your findings will directly influence the next generation of our products. ● Work closely with software engineers, product managers, and fellow scientists to translate research into impactful features. ● Foster and lead partnerships with academic institutions to drive joint research initiatives and publish findings in top-tier conferences and journals. ● Leverage your deep technical expertise in generative AI, distributed systems, and modern machine learning frameworks to build robust, scalable, and cutting-edge solutions that redefine user experiences. Qualifications ● Demonstrated research success through publications in premier AI/ML conferences, patents, or significant open-source contributions. ● Deep, hands-on expertise in one or more of the following areas: LLM fine-tuning (e.g., SFT, RLHF, DPO), generative audio/speech models (TTS/STT), direct Speech-to-Speech (STS) models. ● Proven experience in designing and building large-scale AI systems in a production environment. ● Proficiency in Python and deep learning frameworks such as PyTorch or TensorFlow. ● Moreover, an ideal candidate will possess a relentless curiosity to learn and a deep-seated drive to build impactful, large-scale systems. ● Should have done one publication or open source contribution and cgpa min. 8 What do we have to offer? ● An exciting and challenging working environment with passionate and enthusiastic people that builds an entrepreneurial mindset. ● We offer a workplace environment that is challenging and rewarding at the same time, with minimal formal structure. ● Be part of a start-up from the very beginning, work directly with founders, lead your area of expertise, and be a part of this exciting growth journey of changing the world.
