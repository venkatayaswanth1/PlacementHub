Job Description

We are looking for passionate and motivated fresh graduates to join our engineering team. As a Software Engineer, you’ll work closely with senior developers to design, develop, test, and maintain scalable software solutions. This is a great opportunity to learn, grow, and contribute to real-world projects in a collaborative environment. Responsibilities: ● Design, code, test, and debug software applications. ● Collaborate with cross-functional teams (Product, QA, DevOps) to deliver high-quality solutions. ● Participate in code reviews and learn best engineering practices. ● Write clean, efficient, and maintainable code. ● Contribute to documentation and continuous improvement initiatives. Requirements: ● Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field. ● Good understanding of data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented programming. ● Knowledge of at least one programming language such as Java, Python, JavaScript, C++, or Go. ● Familiarity with version control (Git) and basic software development lifecycle concepts. ● Strong problem-solving and analytical skills. ● Eagerness to learn new technologies and frameworks. Nice to Have: ● Exposure to web development, cloud platforms, or databases. ● Internship or project experience in software development.
