Job Description

As a full time employee, you will work with the leadership team to enhance existing systems or processes, identify improvement areas and reduce operational inefficiencies to better serve DTDC's customers. Your responsibilities will include analyzing data, deriving meaningful insights, creating models, interacting with cross-functional teams, recommending solutions and piloting improvements in line with our vision of becoming the most admired express logistics company in India. You will be assigned one of several key projects based on business needs, including Operations Excellence, Data Analytics/Science, Geospatial Analytics, Operations Research, Operations Management. Your role will involve developing, reviewing, and/or executing logic and analytical solutions for business agendas. This program offers the opportunity to explore DTDC’s globally recognized network and standards. You will build your capabilities through hands-on experience, mentoring, and training from passionate and experienced professionals in a dynamic and respectful work environment. We are seeking high-potential, flexible, innovative, and forward-thinking professionals ready to drive high-visibility, strategic, revenue-generating projects and lead operational process improvement initiatives using Lean methodologies. We are seeking individuals who are prepared to delve into the intricacies of our operations, contributing to our pursuit of excellence. If you are ready to take on this challenge and help us elevate our standards, we warmly invite you to join our team. Preferred Qualifications
