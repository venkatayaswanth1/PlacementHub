Job Description

Associate Product Manager – Internship + Pre-Placement Offer (PPO) Location: Bengaluru / Remote (as applicable) Duration: 6-Month Internship → Guaranteed Full-Time Placement on Successful Completion About Signzy Signzy is a digital trust infrastructure company transforming how businesses authenticate and onboard their customers. We provide advanced identification, background verification, forgery detection, and contract management systems that make contracting trustable, safe, legal, and frictionless. Our biometric authentication systems and blockchain-backed digital audit trails ensure compliance and non-repudiation, strengthening enforceability in legal environments. We are a tech-driven team backed by world-class investors who are passionate about building cutting-edge AI and digital trust solutions. Working at Signzy At Signzy, we breathe software. We constantly experiment with the latest technologies to build next-generation digital trust products. Our culture encourages curiosity, ownership, and innovation. We are now inviting talented students to join us as Associate Product Manager Interns—future product leaders who will help bring futuristic ideas to life. About the Role This is a unique opportunity designed exclusively for campus hires. You will join us as a Product Management Intern for 6 months. During this period, you will work closely with experienced Product Managers, engineers, and business teams to understand real-world product development. Upon successful completion of the internship, you will receive a confirmed full-time Associate Product Manager offer at Signzy. What You’ll Do (During Internship & Full-Time Role) Work with the Product Team to support product strategy including pricing, user segments, and partner ecosystem. Assist in requirement gathering, product design, development workflows, and lifecycle management. Collaborate with business teams and stakeholders to define and refine scope, timelines, and product enhancements. Support deployment of new products for early customers and help track product performance. Partner with the QA team to ensure high-quality releases that match product specs. Coordinate with distribution and sales partners to understand market opportunities. Conduct market and competitive research to identify trends, product gaps, and innovation opportunities. Work with content and design teams on product campaigns and communication. Contribute to iterative improvements in the product portfolio to strengthen competitive positioning. What You’ll Bring Final-year engineering students (B.Tech/B.E.) or recent graduates. Strong analytical and structured thinking abilities. Keen interest in product management, digital products, fintech, and emerging tech. Ability to derive insights from primary and secondary research. Empathy and stakeholder understanding—users, engineers, sales, and partners. Clear communication skills, especially in writing workflows, feature definitions, and product documentation. Why Join Us Be part of a fast-growing, well-funded startup building the global standard for digital trust. Work directly with experienced PMs, founders, and tech leads. Opportunity to own high-impact product areas early in your career. Culture built on respect, empathy, transparency, and collaboration. Guaranteed full-time placement (Associate Product Manager) upon successful completion of the internship. A high-growth environment with opportunities to transition into senior product roles.
