Overview of the Business: At American Express, our Risk management and decision science teams play a pivotal role in powering the company’s global strategy through advanced analytics and machine learning. We leverage vast, rich datasets to develop innovative solutions that drive critical business decisions across credit and fraud risk management, customer personalization, marketing, and Operational efficiency. Our teams work at the intersection of cutting-edge technology and business strategy, building AI / ML models and analytical tools that directly impact millions of customers worldwide. This is a place where data scientists and analysts are empowered to think courageously, experiment responsibly, and deliver insights in close collaboration with industry’s top leaders. If you're passionate about solving real world business problems and creating solutions that impact millions, you should consider a career in Amex. Specific job responsibilities will depend on a combination of the business problem and job family. Business problems span across multiple dimensions. An indicative list is given below: 1. Marketing: Effective targeting for customer acquisition & merchant marketing campaigns and drive engagement across channels to maximize return on investments (Roles) 2. Underwriting: Assessing creditworthiness of applicants and establishing profitable acquisition 3. Customer Management: Dynamically assessing customer behavior to take appropriate lending decisions. 4. Fraud Prevention: Preventing misuse of genuine customers’ information by fraudsters 5. Customer Experience: Reducing friction and elevating customers’ interactions with AXP 6. Loyalty & Engagement: Acquire new customers, increase loyalty and engagement through framework innovative product and capabilities. 7. Digital Platforms: Development, review and/or execution of analytical solutions to improve prospect and customer experience on AXP digital assets including website, mobile app, email, and push notifications. 8. AI Labs: Explore and deploy emerging algorithms and technologies to establish model excellence and enable advanced analytics. 9. Finance Analytics: Revolutionize decision-making in Finance, leveraging cutting-edge, data-driven techniques to deliver insights at scale, powering our growth engine and effectively managing P&L performance. 10. Independent Risk Management: Define the framework by which risk should be managed and additionally provide independent review, challenge, monitoring, and oversight of front-line activities to ensure adherence to risk framework. There are three job families within the Analytics, Data Science and AI / ML Research & Development teams, each of which contribute to solving the business problems mentioned above: 1. Decision Science: Development, deployment and validation of predictive model(s) and supporting use of models in business strategies to enable profitable decisions across risk, fraud and marketing 2. Business and Analytical Solutions: Development, review and/or execution of business strategies/analytical solutions to drive profitable marketing or credit business actions. Generally, the role will entail the following responsibilities: 1. Understand American Express’s core business and what drives various decisions. 2. Understand a modelling domain (Risk / Credit / Fraud / Marketing) and actively contribute towards developing, refining and implementing machine learning (ML) models. Leverage advanced ML techniques to innovate for the next generation of models and drive business growth. 3. Bring in ideas by incorporating external perspectives and identify appropriate use cases to enhance model development and innovation. 4. Collaborate with relevant stakeholders to ensure alignment of analytical and modelling efforts with business objectives, effectively managing stakeholder expectations and communication throughout the project lifecycle. Conduct case reviews to generate insights for improving the model performance and decision-making processes. Must haves- Required Skills 1. Ability to integrate with Cross-Functional Business Partners Worldwide. 6. Ability to drive project deliverables to achieve business results and work effectively in a team environment.
