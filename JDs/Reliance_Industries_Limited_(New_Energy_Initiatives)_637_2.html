Job Description – Manager (Electrical / Instrumentation / Electronics) Reliance New Energy Limited Reliance is building the world's most comprehensive, integrated, and future-ready New Energy ecosystem to drive India's journey towards energy independence and global sustainability leadership. As part of our strategic commitment, Reliance has pledged to achieve Net-Zero Carbon status by 2035 — one of the most ambitious climate goals globally. We aim to enable India’s shift from an energy-importing nation to a global clean-energy powerhouse and empower MSMEs, startups, academia and industry partners to participate in the green-tech ecosystem. Our New Energy Giga Complex spans Navi Mumbai, Jamnagar, Dahej, Kutch and Bengaluru with full-stack engineering, commissioning, R&D, and operations capability. Work Locations (Full-time office): Navi Mumbai, Jamnagar, Dahej, Kutch, Bengaluru and PAN-India project sites.
