Skyroot Aerospace – Engineering Roles (Mechanical / Aerospace / Industrial Engineering) About Skyroot Aerospace Skyroot Aerospace is India’s leading private space launch vehicle company, founded with the vision of “Opening Space for All.” We are pioneering India’s private space revolution by developing cutting-edge launch vehicle technologies that enable cost-effective and reliable access to space. Our team is driven by innovation, technical excellence, and a shared passion for space exploration and engineering breakthroughs. Role Overview We are looking for highly motivated engineering graduates to join our dynamic engineering teams. You will work on challenging, multidisciplinary projects that contribute directly to the design, development, testing, and production of next-generation space launch systems. This opportunity provides a platform to apply your core engineering knowledge to cutting-edge, real-world aerospace applications, while working alongside some of the best minds in the industry. Roles and Responsibilities • Participate in design, analysis and validation of advanced space systems and components. • Support development, manufacturing, integration, and testing activities across multiple domains. • Work collaboratively with cross-functional teams to solve complex engineering challenges and deliver innovative solutions. • Contribute to continuous improvement, optimization, and innovation in product development. • Ensure compliance with aerospace quality, safety, and reliability standards. Must-Have Qualifications • B.Tech/B.E. (Final year or recent graduate) in Mechanical, Aerospace, Aeronautical, or Industrial Engineering. • Strong fundamentals in core engineering subjects such as Solid Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Heat Transfer, Material Science, and Fluid Dynamics. • Proficiency in engineering tools and software (CAD, FEA, CFD, C/C++, Python or similar) • Strong analytical mindset, innovation-driven problem-solving, and attention to detail. • Excellent teamwork, communication skills, and a proactive, self-driven learning attitude. • Genuine passion for aerospace and a strong drive to build technologies that shape the future of space exploration. Why Join Skyroot Aerospace? • Be part of India’s first private company to build and launch space vehicles. • Work on end-to-end development of real space systems with a young and dynamic team. • Mentorship from experienced engineers and scientists, including ISRO alumni. • Collaborative, high-growth environment where your ideas and work have real impact. Location: Hyderabad, Telangana Employment Type: Full-time Eligible Disciplines: Mechanical / Aerospace / Aeronautical / Industrial Engineering Role Overview We are seeking highly motivated and technically strong graduates to join our Mission and Guidance, Navigation & Control (GNC) teams. You will work on end-to-end development of Trajectory design, GNC algorithms, flight software and perform various mission studies for launch vehicles. This role provides an exceptional opportunity to apply your flight dynamics, control system design, sensor fusion and electronics expertise to real-world aerospace applications in a fast-paced, innovation-driven environment. Roles and Responsibilities • Work on the design and development of Launch Vehicle Mission design, GNC algorithms and software, six Degree of Freedom launch vehicle models and mission simulations. • Support integration and validation of GNC software in the flight computer through simulations and ground tests. • Perform mission studies to ensure the robustness of the software and safety of the launch vehicle. • Collaborate across teams (mechanical, propulsion, avionics) to ensure seamless system-level performance. • Ensure compliance with aerospace quality, safety, and reliability standards. Must-Have Qualifications • B.Tech / B.E. (Final Year or Recent Graduate) in Aerospace Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering or Instrumentation and Control Engineering. • Strong fundamentals in flight mechanics, control systems, sensor fusion and embedded software development. • Proficiency in C/C++, MATLAB and Python, and exposure to microcontrollers, FPGAs, or embedded platforms. • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and debugging skills. • Excellent teamwork, communication, and a passion for technology and aerospace innovation. Why Join Skyroot Aerospace? • Work on cutting-edge Mission Design and GNC systems that power India’s private space missions. • Collaborate with experienced engineers, scientists, and ISRO alumni. • Opportunity to see your designs and code literally take flight. • A learning-driven, collaborative culture focused on engineering excellence and innovation. Location: Hyderabad, Telangana Employment Type: Full-time Eligible Disciplines: Aerospace Engineering/ Electrical and Electronics Engineering/ Instrumentation and Control Engineering. Role Overview We are seeking highly motivated and technically strong graduates to join our Avionics team. You will work on end-to-end development of flight-critical electronic systems, embedded software, and electrical integration that power our space launch vehicles. This role provides an exceptional opportunity to apply your electronics and software expertise to real-world aerospace applications in a fast-paced, innovation-driven environment. Roles and Responsibilities • Contribute to the design and development of embedded systems, electronics hardware, and software for flight and ground systems. • Work on hardware design, PCB development, firmware programming, and system testing. • Support integration and validation of avionics systems including sensors, controllers, and power distribution. • Participate in data acquisition, telemetry, and control system development for launch vehicles. • Collaborate across teams (mechanical, propulsion, systems) to ensure seamless system-level performance. • Ensure compliance with aerospace quality, safety, and reliability standards. Must-Have Qualifications • B.Tech / B.E. (Final Year or Recent Graduate) in Electronics and Communication, Electrical, or Instrumentation Engineering. • Strong fundamentals in electronics, control systems, embedded systems, and circuit design. • Proficiency in C/C++ or Python, and exposure to microcontrollers, FPGAs, or embedded platforms. • Knowledge of electrical schematics, PCB tools, or simulation software (MATLAB, LTSpice, etc.). • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and debugging skills. • Excellent teamwork, communication, and a passion for technology and aerospace innovation. Why Join Skyroot Aerospace? • Work on cutting-edge avionics that power India’s private space missions. • Collaborate with experienced engineers, scientists, and ISRO alumni. • Opportunity to see your designs and code literally take flight. • A learning-driven, collaborative culture focused on engineering excellence and innovation. Location: Hyderabad, Telangana Employment Type: Full-time Eligible Disciplines: Electronics and Communication / Electrical / Instrumentation Engineering
