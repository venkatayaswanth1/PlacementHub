Job Description

Role Overview: We are looking for a dynamic professional to join the Founder’s Office – a high-impact role that works directly with the leadership team across all critical functions. This is a multi-hat position that combines problem solving, system building, and strategic execution. The role demands agility to tackle urgent challenges, foresight to design preventive solutions, and ownership to drive key initiatives that shape the company’s growth. 1. System Building & Process Strengthening Identify recurring issues and design preventive solutions to reduce future crises. Establish SOPs, checklists, and review mechanisms across Sales, Tendering, BD, and Projects. Monitor key metrics and early warning signals to proactively flag risks before they escalate. Support leadership in strategic planning, execution tracking, and governance. 2. Problem Solving & Crisis Management Address urgent challenges and unblock teams when critical issues arise. Troubleshoot operational bottlenecks across divisions and drive quick resolutions. Act as a bridge between leadership and functional teams to ensure alignment in high-pressure situations. Manage escalations from clients, vendors, or internal teams with speed and clarity. 3. Strategic Projects & Growth Enablers Lead and execute high-priority initiatives directly driven by the Founders. Translate strategic goals into actionable project plans with clear milestones and ownership. Ensure timely execution, progress tracking, and stakeholder alignment for these initiatives. Deliver measurable impact through focused execution and regular reporting to Founders.
