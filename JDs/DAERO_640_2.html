Job Description

DAERO is looking for a Software Engineer to own the entire product development lifecycle for our Android App - from system design and UI architecture to backend integration. You’ll drive A/B testing, ensure high reliability across devices, and make DAERO’s app reactive, scalable, and loved by our users. Responsibilities Build and maintain scalable Android systems using Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, and Gradle. Implement A/B testing, unit tests, and performance checks for all types of android devices, especially lower-end ones. Collaborate on backend APIs, microservices, and integration workflows. Participate in system design discussions to ensure long-term scalability. Requirements You love Kotlin/Android App development, and have stood up several apps before. Deep understanding of Jetpack Compose, Gradle, and Android performance tooling. Strong foundation in System design and API integration. Experience with testing frameworks and CI/CD. Exposure to AWS and Supabase preferred. Our Values My work is my craft: The best work happens in between the lines. We obsess over details. All boats rise: We do everything to make our customers succeed. It’s the only way we succeed. Low ego, high integrity: We are a small and mighty team. We rely on each and every team member to provide significant coverage.
