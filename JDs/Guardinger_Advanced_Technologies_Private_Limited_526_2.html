Job Description

Key Responsibilities • Develop and implement advanced machine learning / deep learning models to analyse sensor, radar, sonar, geospatial and other defence-related data streams. • Work with multi-modal data (e.g., time-series, acoustics, imagery, geophysical) and build pipelines for preprocessing, feature extraction, and model training/validation. • Develop algorithms for signal processing, anomaly detection, classification, tracking, object recognition, and sensor fusion. • Collaborate with hardware and embedded teams to deploy analytic models on edge devices or sensor platforms. • Perform research and proof-of-concept studies, evaluate state-of-the-art academic methods and adapt them into the company’s product stack. • Generate actionable insights and reports from data; present findings to engineering, product and defence-stakeholder teams. • Ensure modelling and analytics conform to industry standards (especially in defence/strategic applications) regarding accuracy, robustness and reliability. Required Qualifications • M.Tech or PhD (from reputed institute, e.g., IITs) in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Data Science, Signal Processing, Applied Mathematics or related field. • Strong background in machine learning, deep learning, statistical modelling, signal/time-series analysis, sensor data processing. • Proficiency in Python (and/or MATLAB), experience with libraries such as TensorFlow/PyTorch, scikit-learn, Pandas, NumPy, etc. • Experience (academic or industrial) with one or more of: radar/sonar/acoustic signal processing, geospatial/remote sensing data, embedded/edge deployments, sensor fusion. • Strong understanding of mathematics (linear algebra, probability, statistics), and ability to translate research into product-grade code. • Excellent communication skills: ability to document research, present technical results, coordinate with cross-functional teams. Preferred Skills • Experience working with edge computing or embedded platforms (e.g., microcontrollers, FPGA, custom sensor modules). • Familiarity with aerospace/defence domain, ruggedised hardware environments, real-time systems. • Experience in handling large datasets, streaming data, real-time analytics. • Publications or project work in signal processing, defence sensor systems, remote sensing or geospatial analytics. • Knowledge of MATLAB/Simulink, LabVIEW, or similar tools is a plus.
