Job Description

About the Role We are seeking dynamic, tech-savvy individuals who are passionate about using technology to simplify complex workflows. As a Digital Solutions Developer, you will design and develop digital tools, automation platforms, and data-driven dashboards that enhance how teams collaborate, analyze, and make decisions. This role suits professionals with a technical background in software development, data engineering, or full-stack development who are eager to apply their skills to real-world digital transformation projects. You’ll work in a collaborative, fast-paced environment that encourages curiosity, experimentation, and ownership — blending creativity with practical implementation. Key Responsibilities • Develop and maintain digital workflow platforms, web applications, and dashboards that connect internal teams, clients, and consultants. • Work with cross-functional teams to automate manual processes using APIs, scripts, and data integrations. • Understand data from multiple sources (spreadsheets, databases, APIs, or project tools) and design systems to visualize and manage them efficiently. • Collaborate with designers and analysts to translate requirements into intuitive, userfriendly digital solutions. • Research and propose new ideas to improve collaboration, automation, and data visibility across the organization. • Ensure quality, scalability, and maintainability in all developed solutions.
