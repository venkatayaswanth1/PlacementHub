Job Description

JAVIS is looking for a Customer Success Manager I who will play a pivotal role in ensuring the success and satisfaction of our clients. You will be responsible for developing and maintaining strong, long-lasting customer relationships, driving customer adoption and retention, and identifying growth opportunities. Responsibilities: ● Lead the onboarding process for new enterprise customers, ensuring a smooth transition and product adoption. ● Work closely with customers to understand their business goals and help them derive maximum value from our product. ● Monitor customer satisfaction and engagement levels, proactively addressing any issues or concerns. ● Develop and maintain customer success plans, outlining goals, metrics, and action steps for achieving success. ● Utilize customer data and analytics to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities to enhance customer success. ● Provide data-driven insights to customers to optimize their use of our product. ● Own and nurture customer relationships, serving as the primary point of contact for their needs and concerns Requirements: ● B.Tech / B.S. / Dual degree in CSE, M&C, EE, AI or equivalent ● JEE Advanced General Rank within the top 6000 is a must ● Strong academic record with a minimum CGPA of 7.5 & above is a must ● Strong analytical skills and proficiency in tools (e.g., Excel and SAP). ● Excellent communication skills and presentation skills. ● Must possess data driven decision making and problem-solving skills. ● Knowledge of Supply Chain Management would be a plus ● Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic start-up environment ● Highly organized, detail-oriented, and capable of managing multiple tasks and priorities.
