Job Description

1. Developing and implementing comprehensive functional qualification and acceptance plans for flight hardware. Collaborate with design and quality assurance teams to integrate hardware qualification activities into the project. 2. Coordinate with designers to evaluate and ensure all pre-test preparations are complete and any identified issues are resolved. Report on the readiness status of each subsystem highlighting any risks or areas needing further attention before proceeding with testing. 3. Find innovative ways to create, review, and optimise test processes/procedures to increase testing capability and rate 4. Development and implementation of testing protocols and procedures. 5. Address day to day engineering issues during test operations 6. Define and execute software verification and validation processes, including unit tests, integration tests, and system level tests. 7. Monitor and manage test checklists to ensure all steps are followed accurately during testing 8. Preparation of reports of the test performed. 9. Identify, log, and track observations during the test using relevant tools. 10. Perform real-time and post-test data review to evaluate the performance of software 11. Maintain comprehensive records of test results and performance metrics. Communicate observations and recommendations to relevant design teams. 12. Work to collaborate with designers to provide feedback and guidance for continuous improvement. 13. Grade the work of designers to ensure quality and compliance to standards. Collect and document reports from the designers on the justification for the observations from the test for further progress. 14. Own and manage all data generated from tests ensuring its accuracy, integrity, and proper documentation. Ensure compliance with data management and confidentiality protocols. 15. Manage team members to perform integrated testing involving all subsystems 16. Perform other related duties, as assigned, for the purpose of ensuring an efficient and effective work environment 17. Serve as the final authority to ensure all operations are conducted in a safe manner
