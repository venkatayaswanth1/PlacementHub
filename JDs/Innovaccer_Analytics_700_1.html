A Day in the Life ● Strategic Market Development: ○ Research industry trends, identify whitespace opportunities, and map strategic needs of enterprise prospects. ○ Analyze prospect organizations to diagnose pain points, workflows, maturity stages, and transformation readiness. ○ Develop hypotheses on where our platform/solutions can deliver outsized value. ● Executive-Level Engagement: ○ Craft tailored outreach that reflects an understanding of the prospect’s business model, priorities, and pressures. ○ Engage with VP–C-level stakeholders in first conversations and discovery sessions. ○ Drive narratives that reframe how prospects think about their problems and decision frameworks. ● Structured Discovery & Problem Definition: ○ Conduct consulting-style discovery calls — probing root causes, quantifying impact, and shaping a structured problem statement. ○ Collaborate with sales and solution teams to translate unstructured executive conversations into crisp opportunity hypotheses. ● Pilot Orchestration & Deal Acceleration: ○ Guide prospects toward aligned pilots/proofs of value. ○ Coordinate internal cross-functional teams to design pilot scopes grounded in business outcomes and ROI. ○ Maintain momentum across long sales cycles by managing stakeholders, surfacing risks, and reinforcing value. ● Collaboration With Enterprise Sales: ○ Operates as an extension of the Enterprise Account Executive team, supporting strategy, research, and executive communications. ○ Support business case creation, competitive positioning, and meeting preparation for high-stakes conversations.
