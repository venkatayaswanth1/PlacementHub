Job Description

Junior Embedded Engineer About Miko: Miko is a Mumbai based advanced consumer robotics innovation lab passionate about bringing the best of technology to young learners everywhere and home to some of the world’s leading engineers, mathematicians, teachers and psychologists. Our robots have been designed for the age group of 5-10 and the company’s flagship product, Miko 3, is the first robot that uses playful, conversational learning to educate, engage and entertain kids and is best-selling in the US, UK and UAE besides India. We are looking for a hardware engineer in the team. In this role, we expect you to have a decent experience in hardware architecture design and development of digital & analog circuits, microprocessors/microcontrollers based peripheral interface, power electronics and embedded systems. Role: We are looking for an Embedded Engineer in the team. In this role, we expect you to have a strong logical reasoning and decent coding skills with expertise in Firmware and Embedded Systems. Basic understanding of Embedded Hardware is essential. Responsibilities: • Designing and developing robot firmware on Embedded devices • Interaction with the hardware team in new development cycles for software optimization • Ensuring timelines of software production releases with maintenance and regular updates. Must Knows: • Expertise in C/C++ with Object Oriented Programming/Classes. • Must have interfaced sensors such as IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit), Time-of-Flight, LiDAR, IR Sensors, Ultrasonics. (At least one of them is a must). • In-depth knowledge of microcontroller and microprocessor peripherals such as DMA, Interrupt Handlers, Memory profilers and debuggers. • In-depth understanding of application code profiling and debugging tools on embedded platforms for active memory consumption and CPU usage. • Good working knowledge of Embedded communication protocols in depth such as SPI, I2C, UART. (At least one of them is a must) • Have working experience on ARM CORTEX microcontrollers: STM32 series/TI MSP series • Knowledge of Linux OS is a must. Added Advantage: • Software filters as well as sensor data fusion algorithms such as Kalman filter • Working experience with PID Controllers or one of the other control algorithms • Knowledge of Java is a plus • Working experience on Digital Signal Processors, ASICs, FPGAs is a plus Experience with computational languages like MATLAB and Octave is a plus. • Good understanding of multithreading approaches in embedded systems • Knowledge of Python is a plus • Have implemented firmware drivers such as IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit), Time- ofFlight, LiDAR, IR Sensors.
