Job Description

Position: Assistant Manager – Technical (Mechanical) Department: Technical Operations / Engineering Location: As per business requirement Reporting To: Respective Reporting Manager Annual CTC: ₹10,00,000 (Ten Lakhs) Annual Holidays / Leaves: 12 About the Role We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Assistant Manager – Technical (Mechanical) to join our engineering team. The ideal candidate will support technical operations, lead maintenance projects, optimize mechanical systems, and coordinate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless execution of engineering activities. Key Responsibilities 1. Mechanical Operations & Maintenance Oversee preventive and corrective maintenance of mechanical equipment and systems. Conduct root-cause analysis (RCA) for mechanical failures and recommend corrective solutions. Ensure downtime is minimized through efficient troubleshooting and maintenance planning. 2. Project Management Assist in planning, execution, and monitoring of engineering and infrastructure projects. Prepare mechanical designs, technical drawings, and project documentation. Coordinate with vendors, contractors, and internal teams to ensure timely project completion. 3. Technical Support & Process Optimization Ensure smooth operation of all mechanical assets and utilities. Identify opportunities for improvement in processes, equipment efficiency, and cost optimization. Evaluate new technologies and support technical upgrades. 4. Compliance & Safety Ensure adherence to safety protocols and statutory mechanical engineering standards. Conduct periodic safety audits and risk assessments. Maintain detailed maintenance logs, inspection reports, and compliance documentation. 5. Team Coordination Collaborate closely with electrical, civil, and operations teams for interdisciplinary tasks. Support junior engineers and technicians with technical guidance. Report daily,weekly, Monthly updates to the Reporting Manager. Required Skills & Competencies Strong expertise in mechanical engineering concepts. Hands-on knowledge of HVAC systems, pumps, compressors, mechanical utilities, etc. Proficiency in CAD tools (AutoCAD/SolidWorks or similar). Strong analytical and problem-solving skills. Good communication, team coordination, and project management abilities. Education & Eligibility B.Tech / B.E. in Mechanical Engineering from a recognized institution. Strong academic background (preferred from IITs/NITs or equivalent). Internship or project experience in mechanical systems, manufacturing, plant engineering, or related fields will be an advantage. Compensation & Benefits CTC: ₹10,00,000 per annum. 12 Holidays/Leaves per annum. Additional allowances and benefits as per company policy. Why Join Us? Opportunity to work on challenging engineering projects. Exposure to modern mechanical systems and technical innovation. Strong career growth and learning environment. Mentorship from experienced engineering leaders.
