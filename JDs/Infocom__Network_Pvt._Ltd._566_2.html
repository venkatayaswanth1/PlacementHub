Job Description

Job Requirements The Role & Responsibilities of the position would include: • Supporting strategy definition for Web, Mobile (Android, iOS) platforms, focusing on short-term enhancements and long-term product evolution. • Collaborating with multi-functional teams to gather, understand, and prioritize product requirements. • Assisting in defining features, preparing user stories, and managing task prioritization for sprints. • Supporting the visualization, conceptualization, and wireframing/prototyping of new product features and enhancements. • Preparing and maintaining Product Requirement Documents (PRDs), process flows, and use case documents. • Supporting sprint-based release management to ensure timely and high-quality product deliveries. • Assisting Go-to-Market efforts, including preparing training materials and supporting demo sessions for internal stakeholders. • Coordinating with the marketing and communication teams for branding support, feature announcements, and product launch content. • Maintaining ownership of basic documentation deliverables such as release notes, FAQs, and product briefs. • Constantly analyzing customer inquiries, onboarding flows, chatbot performance, and automation outcomes to recommend improvements. Qualification • 0–2 years of professional experience in Product Management, Business Analysis, or related roles. • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Business Administration, or a related field. (MBA preferred but not mandatory.) • Freshers with strong internships/projects in product management, UX, or business analysis are encouraged to apply. Ideal Candidate Profile • Strong logical thinking, analytical skills, and ability to break down problems. • Comfort with ambiguity, change management, and fast-paced environments. • Basic understanding of Agile product development and sprint management. • Ability to perform user research, competitive analysis, and basic market research (primary and secondary). • Good communication and documentation skills with the ability to convey complex ideas simply. • Passion for technology, B2B marketplaces, and digital transformation tools like Chatbots and Automation. • Eagerness to learn about emerging tech trends, customer experience, and product design best practices. Why Join Us • Get hands-on exposure to large-scale B2B digital product management. • Opportunity to work with senior leaders and cross-functional teams. • Learn the end-to-end lifecycle of B2B product platforms. • Fast-track career growth in one of India’s largest online marketplaces.
