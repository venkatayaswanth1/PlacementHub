Job Description

About RISA Labs At RISA, we are building the future of mission-critical workflows by leveraging the latest in AI. Our purpose is to solve humanity’s hardest hurdles, and we are starting with the incredibly high-stakes challenges within oncology. We’re not just selling software; we are delivering a new kind of resilient, autonomous infrastructure that powers foundational institutions and ultimately helps accelerate human progress. Founders RISA was founded by Kshitij Jaggi and Kumar Shivang, IIT Kanpur alumni with a proven track record from their previous healthcare startup, Urban Health. Their vision is to streamline oncology care through cutting-edge technology. Funding RISA Labs is backed by $3.5 million in seed funding, led by Flipkart co-founder Binny Bansal, with support from Oncology Ventures, General Catalyst, z21 Ventures, Odd Bird VC, and angel investor Ashish Gupta. The Opportunity As a Member of Technical Staff at Risa Labs, you’ll be part of the core engineering team building scalable, high-impact systems that power our healthcare AI solutions. We’re looking for bright, curious minds in engineering with strong foundations in Python, data structures, algorithms, and system design. This role is ideal for fresh graduates who are eager to solve challenging problems, work on distributed systems and backend technologies, and learn hands-on in a fast-moving startup environment. If you enjoy turning ideas into real, impactful products and thrive in a culture of collaboration and innovation, this is the place to start your journey. What You’ll do - Design, Develop, and Deploy scalable software systems with strong emphasis on Python-based applications and backend engineering - Collaborate cross-functionally with product managers, researchers, and engineers to translate complex problems into elegant, efficient solutions - Contribute to System Design discussions and assist in building reliable, high-performance architectures - Write clean, testable, and maintainable code following best practices in coding standards, version control, and CI/CD - Debug, optimize, and improve system performance, ensuring solutions meet both technical and business requirements - Learn and grow continuously through mentorship, technical reviews, and exposure to cutting-edge problems in healthcare and AI systems What You’ll Bring - Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Information Technology, or a related field - Strong foundation in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and NLP, with hands-on experience in developing or deploying LLM-based systems - Practical exposure to model fine-tuning, prompt engineering, and optimization for performance and scalability - Proficiency in Python and common ML frameworks (e.g., PyTorch, TensorFlow) - Knowledge of system design fundamentals (distributed systems, APIs, databases, cloud concepts) - Strong problem-solving and analytical skills with ability to break down complex problems into structured solutions - Experience with projects/internships involving backend systems, scalable applications, or research-oriented software development is a plus - Eagerness to learn, collaborate, and innovate in a fast-paced startup environment
