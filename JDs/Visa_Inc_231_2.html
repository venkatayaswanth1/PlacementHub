Job Description

Roles & responsibilities: • Your role will require you to develop code for highly scalable and mission critical applications and platforms that are the backbone of the payments ecosystem. You will have the opportunity to use various technologies and Programming Languages (e.g., C++, Golang, Java, Python, JavaScript etc.) • Requirements – Understand requirements from stakeholders (such as Product teams, Project Leads, Architects etc.). Measure the value a customer will receive from a product feature and provide feedback on how a project requirement can enrich this value. Works closely with project/team leads to learn how work is broken down and how estimates are produced. • Design – Work with project leads and architects and learn how a solution is designed. Contribute to design making and design technical solutions that are customer focused and robust. Apply design patterns and principles where applicable. • Code and Test – Build high quality code and its associated test cases using project requirements, hypothesis. Follow coding guidelines and best practices as followed in your teams and identify opportunities where code can be reused. Write code that is modular, easy to understand, well documented, easy to debug, and maintainable. Learn on how design, code and test design reviews happen and actively participate, collaborate and contribute in such reviews. Be a champion and raise the bar of reviews and coding, testing standards. Build robust test cases and automation scripts that help provide a broad test coverage of your code. Integrate test your code where applicable to ensure end-to-end flow is working as expected. Fix any code bugs in due time. • Implementation and Implementation Support – Work closely with the operations and devops teams to implement your project, build dashboards that can help in easy monitoring of deployment and build alerts that highlight any unexpected product behavior or failure. Where/When required, post implementation, on a rotational basis support the deployment on on-call basis. Identify problems and RCA and provide solutions and mitigations on simple issues. • Operational and Engineering Excellence – Participate in retrospectives where applicable and provide your feedback that helps in optimizing or setting-up new processes. Learn from others experiences and feedback and knowledge. Champion engineering excellence by identifying opportunities on how we can increase our resilience, throughput, availability, observability and performance of our products and platforms. • Innovation – Contribute to our deep culture of innovation and continue your quest of learning and applying new cutting-edge technologies such as GenAI Technical Exposure & Skills • Database Systems: General understanding of database systems, including relational databases (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL) and NoSQL databases (e.g., MongoDB, Cassandra). • AI/ML Fundamentals: Basic knowledge of AI/ML concepts and techniques, including supervised and unsupervised learning, model evaluation, and feature engineering. • Generative AI & Language Models: • Familiarity with language models, context management, and prompt engineering. • Exposure to generative AI tools such as coding assistants (GitHub Copilot, Aider, Cline) and their application in software development workflows. Qualifications: So, what are we looking for in a New Grad - Software Engineer? • Recent college graduates with an undergraduate/master’s degree looking to start working in 2026 • Pursuing a Bachelor's/master’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or Computer applications and its related field is preferred. • Proficiency in at least one programming language or technology including, but not limited, to Java, C/C++, C#, .NET, python, Javascript, HTML, CSS and Objective-C. • The ability to take on challenges and address problems head-on • Highly driven and resourceful • Strong ability to collaborate
