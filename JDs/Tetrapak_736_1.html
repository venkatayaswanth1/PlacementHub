Job Description

Future Talent Program is a Trainee Program within Tetra Pak. We offer a permanent position to start on 1st July 2026. You will onboard an individual designed development program for 24 months connected to a specific position and the Tetra Pak organization. During the program you will participate in trainings and job rotations throughout various parts of Tetra Pak to explore your potential and grow as a person. As a Trainee here with us, you will get to know our entire business and collaborate with colleagues all over the world. And this is just the beginning of your Tetra Pak career! From day one you will be delivering results as a true team member following a development plan that is specifically designed for you and your position. The plan includes a combination of project-rotations, international assignments, work within your own department as well as formal training and mentorship from a network of colleagues all over the world. The program will give you broad insight into our company, our culture and our values. After completing this program, you will have gained knowledge, built relationships, and had experiences to help you continue your exciting career within our company. FUTURE TALENT PROGRAM – SALES ANALYST We are looking for a talented business graduate who is passionate & go getter who after the completion of program would be responsible for acquiring new customers in line with the market growth roadmap. In this role, the candidate will develop pipeline of new prospects and deliver on top line and bottom line growth and build strong customer partnerships. This is an individual contributor role, and the position will be based in Pune and report into the Business Development Director – South Asia Markets, Full time- Permanent role. The program starts on July 1st 2026. WHAT YOU WILL DO Key Responsibilities: • Develop and execute service sales strategies to achieve revenue targets. • Identify customer needs and propose appropriate service solutions. • Build and maintain strong relationships with key stakeholders in customer organizations. • Collaborate with internal teams (technical, operations, finance) to ensure seamless service delivery. • Monitor market trends and competitor activities to identify new opportunities. • Prepare and present proposals, quotations, and service contracts. • Ensure customer satisfaction and follow up on service performance. WHO YOU ARE • Mtech /MBA with Dairy or Agriculture or Marketing or Management background. • Bachelor's degree in core engineering disciplines such as Dairy ,Agriculture ,Food or similar. • Sales experience preferred with track record of generating new business • Understanding of local market / customers / categories to identify new opportunities • Strong communication and presentation skills with experience of dealing with C level executives • Value based sales approach with sound techno-commercial acumen • Self-driven, hands on individual and a go getter WE OFFER YOU • A variety of exciting challenges with ample opportunities for development and training in a truly global landscape • A culture that pioneers a spirit of innovation where our industry experts drive visible results • An equal opportunity employment experience that values diversity and inclusion • Market competitive compensation and benefits with flexible working arrangements
