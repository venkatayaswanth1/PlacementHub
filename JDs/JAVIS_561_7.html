PLACEMENT Machine Learning Engineer I (Vision AI) 3000000 INR

Job Description

JAVIS is looking for a Machine Learning Engineer I (Vision AI) who will build cutting-edge computer vision and multimodal AI capabilities that power the next generation of enterprise automation. You will work on extracting intelligence from images, documents, videos, and real-world visual data to transform how companies operate. Responsibilities: ● Source, clean, and pre-process image, video, and document datasets for computer vision model development ● Design, implement, and train vision-based models using techniques such as CNNs, Vision Transformers (ViT), OCR, object detection, and multimodal architectures ● Build and optimize multimodal workflows that combine vision, language, and structured data to automate enterprise use cases ● Deploy vision models into production using cloud platforms, containers, or serverless architectures, ensuring scalability and resilience ● Evaluate models using appropriate metrics (IoU, mAP, accuracy, latency, robustness, etc.) and iterate for performance improvements ● Monitor model performance in production, fine-tune models as needed, and ensure they scale with increasing data and enterprise workloads ● Collaborate with other members of the AI Team to integrate visual intelligence into broader AI systems Requirements: ● B.Tech / B.S. / Dual degree in CSE, M&C, EE, AI or equivalent ● Solid understanding of machine learning, deep learning fundamentals and computer vision concepts ● Proficiency in Python, with hands-on experience in PyTorch, OpenCV, Hugging Face ● Understanding of key vision architectures (CNNs, ViTs, OCR pipelines, detection models) ● Must possess strong analytical, debugging, and problem-solving skills
