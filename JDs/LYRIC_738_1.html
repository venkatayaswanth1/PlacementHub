Job Description

As an Associate Supply Chain Consultant at Lyric.tech, you will play a key role in supporting client engagements focused on supply chain transformation and digital enablement. The role combines data-driven problem solving, process design, and technology implementation to deliver measurable business outcomes. ● Support supply chain strategy, planning, and transformation projects across industries. ● Analyze operational data and identify opportunities for cost optimization, improved efficiency, and agility. ● Assist in process diagnostics, benchmarking, and solution design. ● Work on implementation of ERP and SCM tools such as SAP, Oracle, Kinaxis, or Blue Yonder. ● Prepare client deliverables including reports, dashboards, and executive presentations. ● Collaborate with cross-functional client and internal teams across operations, procurement, finance, and logistics. ● Stay updated on global supply chain trends and emerging technologies.
