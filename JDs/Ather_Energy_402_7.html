PLACEMENT Design Engineer - Hardware Packaging 1500000 INR

Job Description

Role in a nutshell: To design high quality vehicle electronics modules packaging by applying best practices and methods such as DFMEA, DFX, DMAIC, DOE and statistical tolerance analyses. To deliver design definitions for prototype and production modules. To root cause and prevent production and field issues ● Design and development of electronic and electro-mechanical systems like dashboard, controller module, sensors, actuators for various applications. ● Testing and Validation of systems designed and interactions between them by understanding the vehicle architecture. ● Responsible for ensuring manufacturability of electronic box build assembly components and drive quality controls. ● Responsible for Electromechanical Interaction and Impact Analysis and experience in Tolerance stack-up. ● Participate in and contribute to DFMEA, DFM, DFA, DFC, DFS, DFR, and component selection/derating reviews carry out RCA on field failures and implement/recommend CAPA. ● Performing Problem solving using industry best practices such as 5-Why analysis, Fishbone diagram, RCA methodologies etc.. ● Any other work assigned by the reporting manager. The responsibilities can be reviewed or changed from time to time as per the guidance of the reporting manager.
