Job Description

As an Analyst in J.P. Morgan's Global Research Center, you will be responsible for conducting in-depth research and analysis to support the Global Research franchise. Your role involves gathering and analyzing data, building financial models, and creating insightful reports and presentations. You will engage with global colleagues, develop market intelligence, and effectively communicate findings to stakeholders. Besides working with J.P. Morgan’s equity research teams, you will also be engaged with other areas of public side research including fixed income research strategy, derivatives, commodities, quant and indices. You’ll make an impact by Research and analysis Understanding and identifying analysis to address key project objectives and initial hypotheses Gathering and analyzing relevant data from a variety of sources, including company reports, the internet, online databases and JPMorgan proprietary content to enable industry & company analysis Building detailed financial industry / company models, reports, graphs and datasets Storyboarding and communication Creating clear and effective products (written reports/presentations) to draw out insights from analyses and recommendations Communicate key arguments and insights effectively in verbal and written format with key stakeholders Stakeholder engagement Proactively collaborate with colleagues in the global sectors, taking part in conference calls and responding to project requests (often client-generated) Market intelligence Develop deep industry, technology, regulatory & company knowledge for the sector
