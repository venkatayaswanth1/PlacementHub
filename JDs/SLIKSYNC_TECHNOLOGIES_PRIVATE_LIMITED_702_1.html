PLACEMENT Software Developer (Mobile) 1700000 INR

Job Description

Role Overview We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated React Native App Developer with experience in building high-quality mobile applications. The ideal candidate will have a strong product mindset, proven experience in scaling apps used by thousands of users. Key Responsibilities Develop and maintain cross-platform mobile applications using React Native. Collaborate with product managers, designers, and backend developers to build robust and scalable mobile solutions. Write clean, efficient, and reusable code while following best practices. Optimize application performance and ensure high-quality UX/UI. Work on app deployment to the App Store and Google Play Store. Debug and fix production issues, and implement timely updates and feature enhancements. Stay up to date with the latest trends and best practices in mobile development. Required Qualifications Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or equivalent from a Tier engineering college. Experience & knowledge of React Native development. Solid understanding of the mobile development lifecycle. Experience integrating RESTful APIs and third-party libraries. Familiarity with state management libraries like Redux, Zustand, or MobX. Good understanding of JavaScript, TypeScript, and mobile app design patterns. Product-focused mindset with experience working in fast-paced product environments. Strong problem-solving and debugging skills. What We Offer A dynamic and collaborative work environment. Opportunity to work on meaningful products that impact real users. Competitive salary and equity options. Opportunity to work with a passionate, talented team and make an impact on the product. Learning and development budget to grow your skills.
