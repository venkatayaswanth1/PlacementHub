Job Description

🛍️ About Meesho Meesho started with a bold idea - to make internet commerce accessible to every Indian, everywhere. A decade later, we’ve grown into India’s largest horizontal e-commerce platform for the next billion users - but the mission hasn’t changed: Democratize internet commerce for everyone. Our name - Meri Shop - reflects who we build for: the small business owner in Bharat. Whether it’s a saree seller in Surat, a reseller in Ranchi, or a first-time shopper in a Tier-3 town - we’re their platform of choice. We’ve helped millions of sellers to go online, with zero commission, lowest logistics cost, and a deeply localized, relatable assortment that mirrors how India shops. But we’re more than just an app - we’re a culture of first principles thinking, humility, and high ownership. We’ve built a company that moves fast, thinks long-term, and empowers people to take big bets early in their careers. Our 10-year journey has been one of experimentation, pivots, resilience, and impact. From being India’s top reseller platform to becoming the first Indian e-commerce company to turn profitable, we’ve done what few thought was possible - all while keeping our users and sellers at the heart of every decision. So if you’re looking to build for Bharat, solve complex problems at scale, and be part of a team that values courage over convention - Meesho is the place for you. 🎓 About the Role: Senior Associate – Business Management Track This is not your average entry-level job. As a Senior Associate, you’ll be thrown into the deep end from Day 1. You’ll get end-to-end exposure to solving 10x problems, owning business charters, working with CXOs, and shaping strategies that directly impact millions of users and sellers across India. You’ll join one of our core business verticals, working in small, high-impact teams. You’ll solve real business challenges, use data like second nature, and move at the speed of a startup. 🧠 What You'll Work On You'll be mapped to one of the following tracks based on your interest and business needs: 1. Category Management Own the P&L of a product category. Design pricing, assortment, and promotions strategies. Decode user behavior and stay ahead of competition Scale categories from 0 to 1 and 1 to 100. 2. User Growth Design experiments and campaigns to bring millions of new users. Optimize performance marketing and growth loops. Use data to craft insights that shape Meesho’s growth engine. 3. Fulfillment & Experience Solve for delight across delivery, returns, and support. Improve metrics like delivery TAT, NPS, cancellations, and more. Work with product and ops teams to scale high-efficiency systems. 4. Business Development Expand strategic categories and seller networks. Identify high-potential brand partnerships. Influence long-term strategic direction and team goals. 5. Monetization Make commerce sustainable by driving ads and seller monetization. Conceptualize, pilot, and scale monetization products. Work cross-functionally with product, analytics, and sales teams. 🧩 Who Should Apply Final year B.Tech students from any stream A curious, analytical mind with strong problem-solving skills Passion for startups, ownership, and fast-paced work Ability to hustle, work cross-functionally, and influence outcomes Excellent written and verbal communication 💰 Compensation & Perks Total CTC: ₹36.25 LPA Fixed Pay: ₹17.25 LPA Joining Bonus: ₹4.75L per year (for 4 years) Perks You’ll Love: 📍 Flexible work culture & hybrid setup 🍕 Food, fun, and vibrant office spaces 🧠 Exposure to leaders & steep learning curve ✨ Impact from Day 1 in one of India’s fastest-growing startups 🏆 Performance-based growth & ownership 🌈 Why Meesho?
