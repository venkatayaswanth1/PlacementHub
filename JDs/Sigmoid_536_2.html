Job Description

About Sigmoid: Sigmoid enables business transformation using data and analytics, leveraging real-time decisions through insights, by building modern data architectures using cloud and open source. Some of the world’s largest Fortune 1000 data producers engage with Sigmoid to solve complex business problems. Sigmoid brings deep expertise in data engineering, predictive analytics, AI/ML, and DataOps. About the Role: We are seeking a Data Analyst who can leverage data to generate actionable insights, solve complex business problems, and support strategic decision-making. The role involves close collaboration with data engineering and data science teams to collect, clean, analyse, and visualise data using the latest tools, technologies, and AIdriven solutions. Key Responsibilities: • Continuously analyse data to identify anomalies, emerging trends, and opportunities for proactive action. • Develop interactive dashboards and visualisations using tools such as Tableau or Power BI to effectively communicate insights • Write clean, well-structured, and well-documented code that aligns with team standards and ensures reproducibility. • Define, track, and analyse key performance indicators (KPIs) to generate actionable insights that drive business improvement. • Collaborate with data engineering teams to enhance data quality, integrity, and accessibility. • Maintain comprehensive documentation of data sources, analytical processes, and reporting methodologies. Key Skills and Expertise: • Strong structured thinking and ability to articulate ideas clearly and logically. • Solid understanding of data structures, data visualization concepts, and analytical techniques. • Proficiency in SQL and visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI. • Working knowledge of programming languages such as Python, JavaScript, or C++ (good to have). • Strong quantitative skills in data wrangling, analysis, visualization, and data modeling. • Exposure to distributed big data processing frameworks (e.g., PySpark, Jupyter) is an advantage. communicate insights • Ability to communicate complex technical concepts in a clear and compelling manner.
