Job Description

About BATL Bajaj Auto Technology Limited (BATL) is a pioneer in electric mobility innovation. BATL’s EV R&D focus spans traction motors, battery technology, power electronics, intelligent systems, and embedded software, ensuring our electric vehicle platforms set benchmarks for performance, reliability, and sustainability. We push the boundaries of innovation, empowering you to think beyond the status quo alongside highly talented teams across motor design, EV electronics, BMS, embedded software, and intelligent systems. As a Graduate Trainee Engineer – BATL R&D, you will join BATL’s elite EV teams, gaining hands-on exposure to next-generation electric mobility systems. This role is designed for top-performing graduates from premier engineering institutes passionate about technology, innovation, and building the future of electric vehicles. You will work across specialized EV domains, including: ➢ EV Electronics & Hardware: Charging systems, Battery Management Systems (BMS), High Voltage & Low Voltage DC Systems, MCUs, DC-DC Converters, PCB Design, EMC Testing. ➢ EV Motor & Motor Control: Traction motor design (HV/High Power Density/Hub Motor/ISG/Hybrid), motor control algorithms, and mechanical design of EV transmission systems. ➢ EV Battery & BMS: Battery technology R&D, fuel cell integration, in-house BMS firmware development, and mechanical design for traction batteries. ➢ EV Testing & Validation: Component and vehicle-level testing, HIL & connected systems, control strategy, cloud analytics, and ML applications. ➢ EEE Integration: System-level integration of electrical and electronic components across EV platforms, offering cross-functional exposure to all vehicle systems. ➢ Intelligent Systems: Embedded software, OS & BL development, IoT systems, functional safety, V&V, and advanced algorithm development for EV applications. Key Responsibilities ➢ Design, prototype, and validate cutting-edge EV hardware and software solutions. ➢ Collaborate with multi-disciplinary teams to develop motors, batteries, electronics, and control systems for next-generation EVs. ➢ Apply model-based design, simulation tools, and software development for EV systems. ➢ Conduct testing, validation, and data analysis to ensure product reliability, efficiency, and safety. ➢ Contribute to innovative solutions that enhance EV performance, energy efficiency, and user experience. ➢ Participate in cross-functional projects to gain exposure to full EV system integration. What are we looking for? ➢ Technical & Analytical Skills: Solid fundamentals in electrical systems, power electronics, control systems, embedded systems, or EV technologies. Familiarity with MATLAB/Simulink, Python/C/C++, PCB design, motor modeling, or EV system simulations is a plus. ➢ Other Attributes: Adaptable and eager to learn across multiple EV domains. Enthusiastic about driving innovation and shaping the future of electric mobility. Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking abilities. What’s in it for you? ➢ Work on world-class EV platforms shaping the future of electric mobility. ➢ Gain exposure to cross-functional EV R&D & Hands-on experience in cutting-edge labs etc. ➢ Opportunity to file patents and contribute to globally recognized innovations. ➢ Be part of a company with a culture of excellence, learning, and career growth
