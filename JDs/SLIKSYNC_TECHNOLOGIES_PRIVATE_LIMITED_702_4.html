Job Description

Role Overview As an Associate Product Manager at Slikk, you will work closely with design, engineering, operations, and business teams to drive product development from strategy to execution. You will own specific problem statements, deliver user-centric solutions, and contribute to shaping product roadmap decisions that impact millions of users. This role is ideal for someone who is analytical, obsessed with user experience, and excited to build products in a fast-paced startup environment. Responsibilities Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define requirements, align on scope, and deliver high-impact product features. Conduct user research, competitor analysis, and gather insights from data to identify opportunities and validate product decisions. Write clear PRDs, user stories, workflows, and success metrics. Run experiments and A/B tests to improve conversion, retention, and overall customer experience. Work closely with UI/UX design to build intuitive, frictionless user flows. Coordinate with engineering to ensure timely and high-quality delivery while managing trade-offs and prioritization. Build dashboards and track KPIs to measure feature performance and identify improvement levers. Monitor customer feedback, marketplace dynamics, and operational constraints to refine product strategy. Communicate updates and decisions clearly to stakeholders at all levels. Required Skills and Qualifications Strong analytical mindset with proficiency in SQL, Excel/Sheets, and comfort working with product analytics tools. Understanding of product development lifecycle, design thinking, and agile processes. Ability to structure problem statements and break them into actionable steps. Excellent written and verbal communication skills with the ability to influence cross-functional teams. Passion for user experience and an eye for detail. Ability to rapidly learn new tools, technologies, and market dynamics. Self-driven, curious, and comfortable operating in ambiguity and high-speed environments.
