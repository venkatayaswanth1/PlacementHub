Job Description

This is an opportunity to join Flipkart's Associate Product Manager (APM) program, an elite two-year rotational initiative designed for young technologists. As an APM, you'll gain comprehensive exposure across Flipkart's core business disciplines, wielding significant responsibility to ship compelling software that delights millions, propelling Flipkart to new heights in Indian e-commerce.
