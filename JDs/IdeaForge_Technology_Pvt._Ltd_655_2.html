Job Description

Develop and execute comprehensive V&V plans for mechanical & electromechanical systems in drones, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards and industry best practices. - Conduct verification activities, including testing, analysis, and evaluation of mechanical / electrical components and subsystems to validate their performance and reliability. - Develop & validate new test set ups and test procedures to enhance effectiveness and efficiency of test procedures/ equipment. - Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including design engineers, system engineers, and quality assurance personnel, to ensure seamless integration of engineered systems - Design and implement test protocols, procedures, and methodologies to assess mechanical & electromechanical system performance, structural integrity, and durability. - Analyze test results, identify discrepancies or areas for improvement, and provide recommendations for design enhancements or modifications. - Maintain accurate documentation of test procedures, reports, and findings, ensuring traceability and compliance with internal and external standards. - Stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and advancements in mechanical engineering to drive innovation and continuous improvement in drone systems. - Meticulous attention to safety protocols and a commitment to maintaining the highest standards of safety in all testing procedures. Qualifications & Skills: - Bachelor''s or Master''s degree in Mechanical/Aeronautical/Aerospace/Automobile Engineering or related field. - Experience/ Knowledge on Verification and Validation of mechanical systems, preferably in aerospace, automotive, or robotics industries. - Experience in system Integration and Real time testing - Strong knowledge of regulatory standards and compliance requirements in Design, operation and safety of UAS - DFMEA, instrumentation, data analysis and test reporting - Knowledge of mechanical testing standards like JSS/JSG, MIL STD, ASTM etc. - Excellent problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work effectively in a collaborative team environment. - Effective communication skills to convey complex technical information and findings to diverse stakeholders.
