Job Description

"Larsen & Toubro (L&T) believes that building extraordinary infrastructure, engineering solutions, and technological innovations begins with building extraordinary leaders. The Management Trainee (MT) Program is a strategic initiative to identify, develop, and nurture high-potential talent who will contribute as future leaders of the organization. The program offers exposure to strategic and challenging business projects, supported by a structured learning journey that blends on-the-job experiences with formal learning interventions. This program is designed for accelerated career progression, empowering trainees to take on impactful responsibilities early in their careers. As part of the Management Trainee Program, selected MTs will gain exposure to challenging projects, structured learning interventions, and fast-tracked career growth opportunities. The program also includes a one-year structured mentorship initiative called MPower, where each trainee will be a part of a guided mentoring relationship with a Senior Leader at L&T, MTs develop strong leadership capabilities alongside their technical skills, gaining practical insights and professional direction that prepare them for future leadership roles at L&T. If you are driven by purpose, passionate about tackling real-world challenges, and eager to learn from some of the finest minds in engineering and management, L&T offers an empowering platform to lead, innovate, and grow. Join us to build the future—and shape your own leadership journey with impact."
