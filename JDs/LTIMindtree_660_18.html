Job Description

Role Overview : We are looking for an experienced Business Analyst to support our SITA Projects, working closely with customers, development teams, and stakeholders to understand and refine requirements. The Business Analyst will play a crucial role in gathering, analysing, and documenting business and system requirements, ensuring the successful development and delivery of SITA products/solutions such as Borders (eGates, Kiosks, eVISA Systems, and Declaration Systems) and Airport Operations and Passengers processing areas. Key Responsibilities : Requirement Analysis & Stakeholder Communication • Work closely with customers and stakeholders to gather, understand, and clarify business requirements. • Act as a liaison between customers and the development team, ensuring all requirements are clearly defined. • Identify gaps or inconsistencies in customer requirements and recommend solutions. Backlog Management & Documentation • Create and maintain a product backlog in Azure DevOps, ensuring all customer requirements are properly captured. Business Unit: CPTS - Europe Education: M.Tech/ B.Tech Specialization in Aerospace Engineering Location: Pan India 3 ©LTIMindtree | Privileged and Confidential 2025 • Conduct refinement sessions with the development team to break down requirements into user stories. • Write detailed functional specifications, user stories, and acceptance criteria. • Ensure all requirements are well-documented for development, testing, and customer approvals. Development Support & Quality Assurance • Review and approve development work to ensure it meets business requirements. • Work with the QA team to validate requirements during testing phases. • Ensure that the team is developing the right features aligned with customer expectations. Process & Workflow Documentation • Create workflow diagrams, process models, and system flowcharts to illustrate how the solutions will function. • Document business processes and technical implementations for future reference. Customer Engagement & Demos • Conduct customer demos to showcase features and progress. • Gather customer feedback and incorporate necessary changes into the backlog. Participation in RFP Response • Conduct detailed requirement gathering and analysis in collaboration with stakeholders, ensuring business needs are accurately captured and translated into functional specifications aligned with RFP goals. • Act as a liaison between business and technical teams, facilitating workshops and clarification sessions to ensure clear understanding of RFP objectives and contribute to solution design and response readiness. Required Skills & Qualifications Essential Skills: • 12+ Years of strong experience in requirement gathering, analysis, and documentation. • Experience working with Azure DevOps, JIRA, or similar backlog management tools. • Ability to create workflow diagrams, process models, and system documentation. • Strong understanding of software development processes and Agile methodologies. • Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills. • Experience in conducting requirement refinement sessions with development teams. • Ability to review and validate development work against business needs. • Prior experience in participating RFP responses. Preferred Skills: • Knowledge in Airport Operations and Passenger processing. • Experience in Border Control, Immigration, biometric authentication, passport verification, and eVisa systems. • Experience in writing technical implementation documents. Qualifications: • Bachelor’s degree in IT, or a related field
