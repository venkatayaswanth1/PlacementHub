Job Description

Junior AI NLP Engineer About Miko: Miko is an advanced consumer robotics innovation lab and deep-tech company founded by young, dynamic entrepreneurs from IIT Bombay. Our mission is to bring Robotics and AI into every consumer home. We are a global deep tech company with offices in the US, UK, and India, and a customer base across 140+ countries. Miko offers the first-of-its-kind "content on subscription" hardware platform for kids, powered by patented technology. Our flagship products, Miko 3 and Miko mini (www.miko.ai), are playful learning robots that engage, educate, and entertain. These robots feature a proprietary emotional intelligence and adaptive personality engine, complex conversational and multilingual AI, and advanced speech/vision recognition built over an autonomous navigation system. The Miko team is an interdisciplinary group of experts spanning electronics, embedded systems, mechanical, robotics and AI engineering, HRI/UI/UX design, cloud/mobile software, content creation, neuropsychology, and global business mavericks. The Role: We are looking for a Jr. AI NLP Engineer in the team. In this role, you will be engaged in the research, design, development and optimization of state of the art natural language processing algorithms and models for creating a robust conversational experience on Miko series of robots. We expect you to have strong coding, mathematical and implementation skills with expertise in python deep learning frameworks. Work Experience & Qualifications: No restrictions on degree/qualifications; only skills matter. CTC: Responsibilities: • Develop production ready frameworks for applied AI algorithms in diverse applications such as language context understanding models, comprehension based question answering and information retrieval systems as well as robot personality modules. • Improvise existing conversational stack, design new features and develop state of the art algorithms for robust language understanding and conversational experience. • Design, implement and optimize large scale data processing and continuous training pipelines for various NLP tasks on a single/distributed GPU system to accelerate model training development cycles. • Consistent coordination and collaboration with the AI backend, Content, Linguistics and QA teams for efficient development cycles and testing for new features and improvements. • Ensuring timely releases, maintenance and scaling of services in the production environment. Requirements & Skills: • Excellent understanding and familiarity with the Pytorch framework. • Proficiency with Transformers architecture based models (Vector embedding models). • Working experience with question answering/information retrieval systems. • Experience with large language models, prompt and context engineering. • Experience with at least one of the large scale dense vector search libraries such as FAISS, SCANN, ANNOY, NGT. • Experience with Intent prediction/Entity extraction systems. • Excellent understanding of lower level fundamentals of deep learning models such as layer details, attention, normalization, backpropagation, and optimizers. • Experience in transfer learning and fine-tuning of BERT models as well as designing architectures from scratch for downstream tasks. • Good understanding of CPU-GPU transfers, CUDA usage, GPU architectures, concepts of pipelining and multiprocessing as well as latency/throughput bottlenecks during training of neural networks. • Ability to read and implement related academic papers in the NLP domain. • Proficiency in machine learning libraries/frameworks such as HuggingFace, Langchain, Llamaindex, Sklearn, Numpy, Spacy, Sentence Transformers. • Proficiency in one of the databases: MySQL/NoSQL, OrientDB, MongoDB. • Experience in data augmentation and synthetic generation techniques in NLP. • Proficiency with Dockers, Linux OS and basic Bash scripting.
