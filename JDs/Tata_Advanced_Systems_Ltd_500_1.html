Job Description

Job Overview: We are seeking highly motivated and adaptable Post Graduate Engineer Trainees (PGETs) to join our dynamic aerospace & defense company. As a PGET, you will embark on a challenging and rewarding journey, gaining hands-on experience and contributing to critical projects across various functions. We are looking for individuals who: • Are passionate about the aerospace & defense industry. • Have a strong desire to contribute to India's technological advancements. • Are eager to learn and grow in a challenging and rewarding environment. As a PGET, you will have the opportunity to: • Work on cutting-edge projects and technologies. • Gain hands-on experience in various functions. • Receive mentorship and guidance from experienced professionals. • Develop your technical and leadership skills. • Build a successful career in the aerospace & defense industry. Roles may include (but not limited to): 1. Manufacturing Engineering: • Establishing engineering baselines, interfacing the activities directly involved in the configuration management processes, controlling configuration items. • Coordinating Change Review Board, implementing and verifying technical feasibility of engineering change proposals and assigning the responsibilities and disposition authority to CRB members 2. Quality: • Conduct quality checks on new products and processes, and maintain and update all relevant quality documentation and reports. • Identify non-conformances, perform root cause analysis, and implement and monitor corrective actions to prevent recurrence. • Collect and analyze quality data, create reports and dashboards to track key metrics and support continuous improvement efforts. 3. Production: • Responsible for planning and scheduling daily production and preparing load charts. Identify and mitigate potential risks and issues within the production schedule & monitor production activities to ensure quality standards are met Skills we are looking for: • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills. • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills. • Adaptability to work in a dynamic and fast-paced environment. • Willingness to learn and take on new challenges. Note: The specific role assigned to you as a PGET will depend on your skills, interests, and the organization's requirements. We value flexibility and adaptability, and encourage you to explore various areas within the company to find the best fit for your career aspirations. Join us in shaping the future of aerospace & defense in India!
