Job Description

Job Description: We are seeking Graduate and Post Graduate Trainee Engineers to join Aarvee Engineering Consultants Ltd. The trainees will undergo structured training and contribute to engineering design, project management, and consultancy assignments across our business verticals including Railways & Metro Rail, Highways & Bridges, Airports, Ropeways, Ports, Water Resources & Irrigation, Water & Environment, Planning & Urban Design, and other infrastructure domains. Key Responsibilities: Assist in planning, design, analysis, and project management activities. Support preparation of DPRs, technical reports, drawings, and project documentation. Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to deliver innovative and sustainable consultancy solutions. Learn and adapt to company systems, tools, and professional practices in engineering consultancy. Desired Skills: Strong fundamentals in core engineering subjects. Analytical and problem-solving abilities. Familiarity with design/analysis software (AutoCAD, STAAD, MATLAB, etc., as applicable). Good communication and teamwork skills. Eagerness to build a career in engineering design and project management consultancy. Location: Hyderabad
