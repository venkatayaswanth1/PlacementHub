PLACEMENT Composite - Structural Analysis Engineer 1500000 INR

Job Description

Position Summary: We are seeking a highly skilled Composite Structural Analysis Engineer to join our Flight Structures team focused on rocket structures. The successful candidate will play a pivotal role in the design, analysis, and validation of composite materials and structures used in rocket systems. This position requires expertise in Strength of materials, Composite mechanics, Finite element analysis (FEA), and a strong understanding of aerospace engineering principles. Key Responsibilities: ● Conduct detailed structural analysis of composite structures using FEA tool, ensuring compliance with safety and performance requirements under various loading conditions. ● Perform linear static, buckling and thermo-structural analysis incorporating the material nonlinearities. Explicit dynamic simulation experience is an added advantage. ● Collaborate with design engineers to develop and optimise composite structures for weight reduction and performance enhancement. ● Perform material property characterization and testing to support design validation and failure analysis. ● Prepare technical reports and presentations to communicate analysis results and recommendations to multidisciplinary teams. ● Participate in design reviews and contribute to the development of best practices for composite material usage in rocket structures. ● Stay abreast of advancements in composite materials and structural analysis methodologies relevant to aerospace applications.
