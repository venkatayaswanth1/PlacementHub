Job Description

As a Research Engineer I, you’ll work on advancing the intelligence layer that powers GENIE, developing new capabilities in LLM reasoning, multimodal understanding, memory systems, and autonomous task orchestration. Responsibilities: ● Research, design, and implement novel architectures and algorithms in large language models, agentic systems or multimodal AI ● Build evaluation frameworks for reasoning, context retention and long-horizon task execution ● Experiment with fine-tuning, model compression and hybrid retrieval architectures ● Develop proof-of-concept systems that push the boundaries of what GENIE’s AI agents can do ● Stay updated with top-tier research (NeurIPS, ICLR, ACL, ICML) and explore real - world adaptations of emerging AI methods ● Collaborate cross-functionally to bring research prototypes into production-scale systems serving enterprise clients Requirements: ● B.Tech / B.S. / Dual degree in CSE, M&C, EE, AI or equivalent ● Strong foundations in machine learning, deep learning and applied mathematics ● Proficiency in Python, with hands-on experience in PyT orch, TensorFlow or JAX ● Understanding of transformer architectures, attention mechanisms and language model internals ● Familiarity with vector databases, retrieval systems or reinforcement learning (RLHF / RLAIF) ● Passion for AI research, generative models and bridging theory with scalable applications
