Job Description

Our Infrastructure Technology teams work primarily in the areas of Compute, Storage, Virtualization, Cloud, Automation and Infrastructure Applications, with languages such as Rust, Python, Bash. Linux is the operating system of choice in the trading & quantitative research space, and experience using Linux is a requirement. As a graduate infrastructure Engineer with Squarepoint, you will have the opportunity to work with one or more of our Infrastructure Group teams. Responsibilities: • Platform Compute & Storage • Provides infrastructure services such as compute, operating systems images, storage, virtualization, backup, configuration management, etc. • Platform Application Services • Architect, build and maintain managed services for the rest of the firm, ensuring reliability, availability and scalability of the platform. • Act as consultants for developers, quant researchers and production support teams, providing advice and support on how to best utilize the infrastructure and services. • The team is composed of the sub teams: Developers Tools, Middleware, Observability and Schedulers Skill Set: • Experience with Git, Linux or containers (Kubernetes) • Experience with Ansible, Chef or Terraform is a plus • Programming with Python required • Exceptional problem solving skills • Strong English communication skills are a must Preferred Courses: • Computer Architecture • Introduction to Embedded Systems • Operating Systems • Distributed Operating Systems • Computer Networks • Communication Networking • Software Engineering
