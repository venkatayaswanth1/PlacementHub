Job Description

Summary: We are building MFT (Medium Frequency Trading) and HFT (High Frequency Trading) systems which are scalable, automated, and extremely fast. We want passionate people to be part of this journey with us. As a Software Developer, you will be part of a collaborative engineering team that develops, optimizes, and maintains the core trading infrastructure. This role offers an exceptional opportunity to apply your computer science knowledge to real-world problems involving high throughput, low latency, and high availability. You will contribute to building systems that process large volumes of market data, execute orders in nanoseconds, and manage complex state across global trading venues. The ideal candidate should have a strong understanding of C++ and low latency coding, be proficient in low level design, and possess a passion for delivering high-quality code. You will work on building low latency frameworks, processing large amount of real time data, and building scalable automated trading platforms. Job Responsibilities: • Design and develop low-latency trading software in C++ for high-frequency trading systems. • Optimize code and algorithms for maximum performance and minimal latency. • Work closely with the quantitative research team to understand and translate trading strategies into code. • Collaborate with other developers to integrate new features and enhancements into existing systems. • Troubleshoot and debug issues in production environments. • Explore and adopt new technologies that can improve trading efficiency and execution quality. • Stay abreast of advancements in trading technology and implement improvements to enhance efficiency. Knowledge, Competency and Skills requirement: • Strong knowledge of data structures and algorithms. • Proficiency in C++ and OS level architecture. • Experience with database management systems. • Passion for financial markets. • Entrepreneurial, enterprising and an agile mindset. •Ability to adapt to a dynamic work environment and show exceptional prowess at managing large impact situations. • Keen to learn, hungry for growth. • Flexibility to work independently and as a part of the team. • Strong quantitative aptitude and high EQ. Preferred Skills: • Understanding of core C++ concepts. • Knowledge of OS, networks, and system architecture. • Strong data structures and algorithms understanding. • Knowledge of low latency design. • Understanding of security principles and best practices in software development.
