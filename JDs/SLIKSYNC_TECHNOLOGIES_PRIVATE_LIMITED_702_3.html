Job Description

Role Overview As a Data Analyst at Slikk, you will be responsible for collecting, analyzing, and interpreting large datasets to uncover actionable insights. You will help optimize merchandising, inventory, customer acquisition, and operational efficiency through data-driven recommendations. This role is ideal for someone who thrives in fast-paced, high-growth environments and is passionate about using data to solve real-world business problems. Responsibilities Work closely with business, product, and operations teams to define metrics, measure performance, and identify growth opportunities. Analyze user behavior, sales trends, and inventory movement to drive actionable insights for marketing and category teams. Build and maintain dashboards and automated reports to support decision-making across functions. Conduct deep-dives into key performance areas (e.g., conversion, retention, delivery performance) to identify root causes and improvement levers. Support demand forecasting, pricing, and assortment planning using statistical and machine learning models. Translate complex data into simple, visual, and compelling stories through dashboards, presentations, and reports. Ensure data integrity by validating sources, cleaning datasets, and maintaining analytical consistency across systems. Collaborate with engineering teams to define data requirements, pipelines, and analytics tracking for new product features. Continuously evaluate and improve data quality, accessibility, and reporting processes.
