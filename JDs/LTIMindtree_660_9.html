Job Description

Job Summary: We are seeking a highly skilled and innovative AI Product Developer to join our team and lead the development of scalable, production-ready AI solutions. You will work at the intersection of software engineering, machine learning, and product strategy to build intelligent systems that solve real-world problems and align with business goals. World is at the cusp of realizing "AI is new UI", this is in the continuum of computing systems evolution. Since the earlier days of computing it was always "learning" to work with computers, be it using input devices, user interface or logic organization of work. This typically lead us to design systems that followed the sequence of input-process-output. With the affordability of AI and ML systems we can now build solutions that start with data, derive conclusions based on the data and learn from the decisions being taken to involve humans in process. This change will enable systems to be autonomous, extremely responsive and limit human intervention to step needed. We are looking for Engineers that can think in the new paradigm, reimagine experiences that will result from this and build systems that can help in realizing the new age solutions.
