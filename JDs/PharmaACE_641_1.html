Job Description

Purpose of the Role: • Join our Data Science & Solutions team to design, build, and deliver AI/ML solutions that inform high-stakes decisions for global pharmaceutical brands. You’ll turn real-world, messy data into clear forecasts, causal insights, segmentations, and concise decision tools—helping therapies reach the right patients faster while learning industry-grade science practices Responsibilities: • Forecasting & Time Series: Contribute to building machine learning models that predict demand using advanced tree-based and neural network models. • Marketing Mix & Causal Measurement: Estimate channel effectiveness (ROI/mROI), run counterfactuals, and build simple scenario simulators. • Segmentation & Prioritization: Cluster HCPs/centers, create explainable profiles, and translate outputs into actioning playbooks. • Applied NLP/LLMs (practical use): Convert unstructured text into features; prototype prompt/eval pipelines where appropriate. • Analytics Engineering: Clean/join claims, CRM and activity data; maintain reproducible notebooks, data checks, and version control. • Decision Delivery: Summarize analyses into clear narratives, dashboards, and client-ready decks; document assumptions and trade-offs. • Quality & Governance: Follow healthcare data privacy and model documentation standards; contribute to code reviews and QA. Qualifications Required: • Bachelor’s/master’s in computer science, Data Science, Statistics, or related discipline. • Proficiency in Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-Learn) and comfort with SQL. • Basics of statistics and machine learning (Regression, Classification). • Ability to work with unstructured data (joins, edge cases) • Good to have: Exposure to models like XGBoost, Random Forest, Logistic, Linear & Bayesian Regression models, Time Series Models (ARIMAx, SARIMAx) Why Join Us: • Applied impact: Your models and analyses inform real world patient outcomes. • Structured growth: Mentorship, onboarding sprints, code reviews, and regular learning. • Breadth & depth: Hands-on work across forecasting, causal, segmentation, NLP, and LLMs • Build to last: Learning how to turn analyses into reusable components & products (apps). • Domain learning: Gain healthcare context alongside strong hands-on data science expertise. Other Competencies: • Strong interpersonal, written and verbal communication skills • Strong attention to detail, with a quality-focused mindset • Analytic problem-solving skills, with a creative and innovative outlook • Independent, creative and driven quantitative thinker • Ability to dive deep into business processes, data, and analytics
