Job Description

About the role Drive operational efficiency across merchant lifecycle management and customer experience. This role is ideal for someone passionate about applying technologies such as AI, ML, and intelligent automation to solve complex business problems. Key Responsibilities Use data and process mining tools to analyze "as-is" business processes, identify inefficiencies, and pinpoint opportunities for automation and optimisation. Support the design, development, and implementation of automation solutions using technologies like robotic process automation (RPA), AI-driven analytics, and machine learning. Work alongside Operations & CS teams to understand their processes, identify improvement areas, gather requirements & implement technical solutions using AI, ML, and process automation Partner with business, product & engineering teams to prioritise feature requests based on operational pain points and provide user feedback for product development. Create and maintain documentation for process workflows, methodologies, and technical specifications for new solutions. Prepare reports and dashboards to track the performance of new processes and communicate results to stakeholders. Assist in training and mentoring end-users on new processes and technologies, fostering a culture of continuous improvement. Real problems that you’ll work on : Establish early warning systems for critical metrics such as settlement delays, success rate degradation, and merchant complaint volume increases Implement Robotic process automation (RPA) solutions for routine tasks such as document verification, basic query routing, and compliance checks, reducing manual effort Design automated workflows for refund approvals, implement risk-based categorization systems, and establish SLAs to reduce processing time from days to hours Improve Merchant Experience - Identify TAT improvement opportunities and improve customer experience metrics. Optimise merchant onboarding workflows and reduce TAT from application submission to account activation Create AI-driven customer support tools, implement smart FAQ systems, and design automated resolution workflows for common merchant issues
