Job Description

We are a global learning platform on a mission to motivate every learner by empowering every eductor. From K–12 classrooms and universities to corporate training settings, our platform is trusted in over 150+ countries by 75 million monthly active users. In the U.S., 90% of schools use Wayground to engage students and transform learning through technology. Wayground is a high-growth, profitable SaaS company backed by leading investors including Tiger Global, GSV, and Nexus Venture Partners. We're a small, passionate, tech-first team solving big challenges in education. We move fast, build with data, and obsess over creating delightful experiences for our global community of educators and learners. We invest in what matters: As an engineer, you will be responsible for designing and building platforms and micro services that must scale horizontally and vertically to power realtime games supporting 1000s of participants in individual games and millions of concurrent games. Our services receive 1000s of requests/sec spread around the world. You will be responsible to improve scaling, monitoring, and observability of existing services and building new services to enable multiple verticals for quizzes. 2+ million quizzes are created every month on our platform. Our systems receive billions of responses/day, and you will be responsible for building systems to reliably process terabytes of data/day to solve meaningful problems for the business. We are looking for passionate backend and frontend engineers to join our growing team to build fast, robust, and efficient systems, services, and data platforms and be a part of making key technical decisions to execute our technical vision as we scale globally.Develop and Optimize: Collaborate with the team to design, develop, and optimize both front-end and back-end components of our applications, ensuring fast performance and a seamless user experience. Participate in the Full Development Cycle: Engage in all stages of the product development process, from initial design and prototyping to coding, testing, and deployment, ensuring high-quality deliverables at every phase. Implement Best Practices: Embrace good engineering practices, including writing clean, maintainable code, and implementing automated testing (unit, integration, and end-to-end) using frameworks such as Jest and Cypress to ensure reliability. You have 0-2 years of experience in software development. You are good at and have some experience in programming with backend OR frontend languages and frameworks like Go, Nodejs, Python, Nextjs, React, etc. You have a drive to learn new technologies and systems, with an interest in developing large-scale, interactive applications. You are passionate about good engineering practices, including automated testing and monitoring and have a keen eye for details in user experience and products.
