Job Description

About the Role We are looking for energetic and analytical young professionals to join our Pre-Sales & Solutioning team as Solution Integrators. This role offers a unique opportunity to learn how complex, enterprise-grade digital solutions are designed, integrated, and presented to global clients. You will work alongside experienced solution architects, technology experts, and business leaders to build winning solution proposals that combine multiple technologies — including Cloud, BPM, Integration, AI/ML, UI/UX, Mobility, SAP, CRM, and Security —into cohesive, value-driven solutions for clients. This position is ideal for students who are passionate about technology, enjoy connecting business challenges to digital solutions, and aspire to grow into Solution Architect or Digital Consulting roles. Key Responsibilities As a Solution Integrator (Pre-Sales), you will: • Support Solution Design: Work with senior architects to conceptualize and design digital transformation solutions across technology domains such as Cloud, BPM, AI/ML, Integration, and Mobility. • Assist in Proposal Development: Contribute to RFP/RFI responses, technical solution documents, and value-based proposals. • Collaborate with Cross-Functional Teams: Coordinate with delivery, domain experts, and pricing teams to bring together technical, financial, and operational components of a solution. • Create Solution Assets: Help document reusable blueprints, accelerators, and reference architecture for future opportunities. • Participate in Client Engagements: Support in preparing solution presentations, demos, and workshops for clients and internal stakeholders. • Research & Innovation: Explore new technologies, trends, and tools that can enhance the organization’s pre-sales and solutioning capabilities. • Commercial Awareness: Develop an understanding of how effort estimation, pricing, and value engineering shape a winning proposal. Job Profile – Senior Software Engineer. What We’re Looking For Educational Qualification • B.E / B.Tech / M.E / M.Tech / MCA – preferably in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related disciplines. • Graduating batch: [2026]. Skills and Attributes • Strong passion for technology, innovation, and digital transformation. • Good understanding of core IT concepts such as: o Application Development (Java, Python, .NET, or Low-code platforms) o Cloud Computing (AWS, Azure, GCP) o APIs, Integration frameworks, and Microservices o BPM / Workflow automation (e.g., Pega, Appian, Camunda) o Data analytics, visualization, and AI/ML basics o UI/UX and enterprise mobility concepts • Analytical mindset with problem-solving abilities. • Strong verbal and written communication skills. • Curiosity to learn business processes and how technology drives enterprise value. • Collaborative attitude and ability to work effectively in diverse teams. Preferred (Nice to Have) • Exposure to cloud or automation platforms through internships or academic projects. • Participation in hackathons, innovation challenges, or open-source projects. • Awareness of enterprise applications like SAP, Salesforce, or ServiceNow. • Basic understanding of solution architecture or pre-sales cycles (through coursework or certifications). Career Path and Learning Opportunities Joining as a Solution Integrator (Pre-Sales) opens multiple growth avenues: • Learn directly from senior architects and pre-sales leaders. • Gain hands-on experience in end-to-end solutioning and proposal building. • Exposure to diverse industries (Banking & Financial Services, Insurance, Healthcare, Technology, Media, Telecom, Logistics & Transportation etc.). • Opportunity to transition into specialized roles such as: o Solution Architect / Technology Consultant o Digital Transformation Consultant o Enterprise Pre-Sales Specialist
