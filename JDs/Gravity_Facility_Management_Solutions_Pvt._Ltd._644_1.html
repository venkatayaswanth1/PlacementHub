Job Description

Position Title: Assistant Manager – Technical (Electrical) Department: Technical / Operations Location: Noida,NCR (Project-Based Deployment) Reporting To: Respective Reporting Manager / Technical Head Annual CTC: ₹10,00,000 (Ten Lakh per annum) Leaves: 12 Holidays per annum + Company-declared holidays Employment Type: Full-Time About the Role We are looking for a highly skilled and motivated Assistant Manager – Technical (Electrical) to oversee and manage technical operations related to electrical systems, maintenance activities, project execution, and site supervision. The candidate will be responsible for ensuring safe, efficient, and compliant operations across assigned locations. This role requires strong technical knowledge, leadership ability, problem-solving skills, and the capacity to manage multiple technical tasks simultaneously. Key Responsibilities 1. Technical Operations & Maintenance Oversee operation, inspection, and maintenance of electrical systems and equipment. Ensure preventive and corrective maintenance schedules are implemented effectively. Monitor breakdowns and ensure timely troubleshooting and restoration. 2. Project Execution & Site Supervision Assist in the planning and execution of electrical projects at various sites. Coordinate with contractors, vendors, and engineering teams for seamless project delivery. Conduct quality checks and ensure electrical work meets technical specifications. 3. Compliance & Safety Ensure all electrical equipment and installations comply with statutory norms and safety standards. Maintain documentation related to safety audits, inspection reports, and compliance records. Implement safety protocols and conduct safety training for team members. 4. Vendor & Team Coordination Assist in vendor management for procurement, installation, and repair works. Coordinate with internal teams and ensure smooth workflow. Support the reporting manager in technical evaluations and decision-making. 5. Reporting & Documentation Prepare technical reports, maintenance logs, and project progress updates. Maintain accurate documentation of electrical operations and equipment data. Submit daily, weekly and monthly status reports to the reporting manager. Required Qualifications & Skills Educational Qualification: B.Tech in Electrical Engineering (Mandatory) Candidates from IIT/Amity or other premier institutions preferred. Technical Skills: Strong knowledge of electrical systems, power distribution, control panels, transformers, DG sets, and HT/LT systems. Understanding of safety standards and statutory compliance (Electrical Act, IE Rules). Ability to read electrical drawings and technical schematics. Soft Skills: Excellent communication and coordination skills Leadership qualities and team-handling capability Good analytical and problem-solving skills Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines Experience: 0–2 years (Freshers with exceptional academic and internship experience may apply) CTC, Benefits & Allowances Annual CTC: ₹10,00,000 Leaves: 12 holidays per annum Travel allowances for outstation assignments (as per actuals) Laptop & official SIM card provided by the company Why Join Us? Opportunity to work on large-scale technical and engineering projects Exposure to leadership-level responsibilities at an early career stage Professional growth, structured development, and industry-level training Work with a fast-growing, innovation-driven organization Application Process Interested students may apply through the IIT Kharagpur Placement Portal. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for: Technical Assessment HR Interview Final Discussion with Reporting Manager / Technical Head
