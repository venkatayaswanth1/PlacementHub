Job Description

About the Role We are looking for a driven and innovative Gen AI & ML Specialist to join Avaada at an exciting inflection point. We are in the nascent stages of establishing our in-house AI capabilities, meaning you won’t just be a cog in a machine—you will help build the machine. In this role, you will work directly with leadership to transform raw ideas into our first generation of in-house AI models and AI-enabled agentic workflows. You will wear multiple hats: researcher, developer, and prototyper, helping us move from "experimentation" to "production" in a fast-paced, startup-like environment within the organization. ________________________________________ Key Responsibilities 1. Building Foundational Gen AI & Agentic Systems • Design and prototype AI Agents that automate complex business workflows using tools like LangChain, AutoGen, or low-code orchestrators (e.g., n8n, Make). • Develop Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems to ground LLM responses in our private company data, ensuring high accuracy and relevance. • Experiment with Prompt Engineering and fine-tuning strategies to customize open-source models (Llama, Mistral) for specific business use cases. 2. Applied Machine Learning & Data Science • Assist in building the company's ML pipelines for tasks such as forecasting, classification, and anomaly detection. • Work on end-to-end data preparation: cleaning unstructured text/documents, creating embeddings, and managing vector databases (e.g., Pinecone, Qdrant, PGVector). • Apply statistical methods to evaluate model performance and ensure outputs are reliable enough for business decision-making. 3. Software Engineering & Deployment • Write clean, modular Python code to turn successful experiments into deployable APIs (using FastAPI/Flask). • Collaborate with the engineering team to integrate AI models into internal web apps and dashboards. • Maintain version control (Git) and documentation, helping establish best practices for the growing data science team. ________________________________________ What We Are Looking For Must-Have (Technical Skills) • Education: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, AI, or a related field. • Core Programming: Strong proficiency in Python. You should be comfortable writing scripts for data manipulation and model building. • Gen AI Fundamentals: Academic or project-based experience with LLMs, Transformers, and frameworks like Hugging Face, LangGraph or LangChain. • Machine Learning: Solid understanding of supervised/unsupervised learning, regression, and deep learning concepts (CNNs/RNNs). • Database Skills: Basic knowledge of SQL and familiarity with vector stores or NoSQL databases. Preferred (The "Builder" Mindset) • Curiosity & Agility: Since we are in the early stages, you must be comfortable with ambiguity and excited to research new tools (e.g., "How do we use an AI Agent to automate this spreadsheet task?"). • Workflow Automation: Exposure to automation tools like n8n, Zapier, or building custom automation scripts is a huge plus. • Cloud Awareness: Basic familiarity with cloud environments (AWS, Azure, or GCP) is helpful but not required. ________________________________________ Why This Role? • High Impact: Unlike established teams where you might tweak one hyperparameter, here you will build entire workflows that define how our company uses AI. • Innovation: We are actively experimenting with Agentic AI and Workflow Automation, giving you exposure to the cutting edge of applied AI. ________________________________________ Application Process To apply, please submit your resume.
