Job Description

You’ll play a key role in supporting the LTF Cloud Integration Platform, ensuring it runs at peak performance. Your mission: identify and enhance availability, resiliency, reliability, and stability across the board. You’ll also provide technical guidance, drive smart solutions, and help shape processes that deliver operational excellence. Skills • Keep our production environment healthy by monitoring availability and maintaining a complete view of system performance. • Design and build software and tools to manage platform infrastructure and applications with ease. • Boost reliability, quality, and speed-to-market for our suite of software solutions. • Measure, analyse, and optimize system performance to stay ahead of customer needs and drive continuous innovation. • Provide core operational support and engineering expertise for large-scale distributed applications. • Track and analyse metrics from operating systems and applications to fine-tune performance and troubleshoot issues. • Collaborate with development teams to make services more reliable through testing, automation, and streamlined release cycles. • Contribute to system design discussions, platform management, and capacity planning. • Create sustainable, automated systems that simplify operations and reduce manual effort. • Balance rapid feature delivery with rock-solid reliability by adhering to clear service-level objectives. • Manage configuration, build-and-release cycles, and CI processes to keep everything running smoothly. Functional Skills ▪ Hands-on programming skills (structured and OOP) in one or more high-level languages like Python, Java, C/C++, .NET, or JavaScript. ▪ Familiar with containerization and orchestration tools like Docker, Kubernetes, or similar technologies. ▪ Proactive in spotting problems, eliminating performance bottlenecks, and driving improvements. ▪ Solid understanding of Linux/Unix systems, networking, and overall system architecture. ▪ Comfortable working with cloud platforms, including deploying and managing applications in the cloud. ▪ Strong analytical mindset for quick troubleshooting and efficient problem resolution. ▪ Bring a positive, can-do attitude and a passion for building great tech.
