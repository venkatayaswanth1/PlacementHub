Job Description

Junior AI Image Processing Engineer About Miko: Miko is an advanced consumer robotics innovation lab and deep-tech company founded by young, dynamic entrepreneurs from IIT Bombay. Our mission is to bring Robotics and AI into every consumer home. We are a global deep tech company with offices in the US, UK, and India, and a customer base across 140+ countries. Miko offers the first-of-its-kind "content on subscription" hardware platform for kids, powered by patented technology. Our flagship products, Miko 3 and Miko mini (www.miko.ai), are playful learning robots that engage, educate, and entertain. These robots feature a proprietary emotional intelligence and adaptive personality engine, complex conversational and multilingual AI, and advanced speech/vision recognition built over an autonomous navigation system. The Miko team is an interdisciplinary group of experts spanning electronics, embedded systems, mechanical, robotics and AI engineering, HRI/UI/UX design, cloud/mobile software, content creation, neuropsychology, and global business mavericks. We are looking for a Jr. AI Image Processing Engineer in the team. In this role, you will be engaged in the research, design, development and optimization of state-of-the-art vision & image processing algorithms and models for creating a robust user experience on Miko series of robots. We expect you to have strong coding, mathematical, signal processing and implementation skills with expertise in python deep learning frameworks. Work Experience & Qualifications: No restrictions on degree/qualifications; only skills matter. CTC: Responsibilities: • Develop production ready edge and cloud frameworks for applied AI algorithms including end-to-end facial analysis pipeline, person identification & re-identification, scene understanding and reconstruction, gesture & activity recognition. • Improvise existing AI & robotics stack, design new features and develop state of the art algorithms such as generative image & video modules, multi-user recognition, facial emotion analysis, scene classification/summarization/remembrance and others. • Design, implement and optimize large scale data processing and continuous training pipelines for various vision tasks on a single/distributed GPU system to accelerate model training development cycles. • Consistent coordination and collaboration with the AI backend, Robotics and QA teams for efficient development cycles and testing for new features and improvements. • Ensuring timely releases, maintenance and scaling of services in the production environment. Requirements & Skills: • Excellent understanding and familiarity with the Pytorch/Tensorflow framework. • Experience with Visual GANs and techniques for synthetic image generation, augmentation, Summarization and enhancements. • Strong understanding of fundamentals of image pre-processing such as normalization, histogram equalization, scaling/interpolation, geometric transformations, color/exposure correction, contrast adjustment, deblurring and denoising. • Proficiency in camera calibration and frame of reference transformations. • Experience with image processing techniques and models such as segmentation, face/person detection, face recognition/person re-identification, tracking and object detection. • Experience with facial landmark extraction and head pose estimation algorithms. • Experience with Large multimodal models with image input, text-to-image and image-to-image models, generative image editing, prompt & context engineering. • Understanding of model compression/optimization/quantization techniques for faster inference on edge/embedded devices as well as cloud GPUs is a plus. • Excellent understanding of lower level fundamentals of deep learning models such as layer details, attention, normalization, backpropagation, and optimizers. • Experience in transfer learning and fine-tuning of deep learning models as well as designing architectures from scratch. • Good understanding of CPU-GPU transfers, CUDA usage, GPU architectures, concepts of pipelining and multiprocessing as well as latency/throughput bottlenecks during training of neural networks. • Ability to read and implement related academic papers in the image processing domain. • Proficiency in data science/image processing libraries/frameworks such as OpenCV, Sklearn, Numpy, Scipy. • Proficiency with Vision Transformers architecture is a plus. • Proficiency with Dockers, Linux OS and basic Bash scripting.
