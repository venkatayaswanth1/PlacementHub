PLACEMENT Loads & Aeroelastics Engineer 1100000 INR

Job Description

Modelling and simulating aircraft aerodynamics, inertia, flexibility, landing gear, control systems, and flight mechanics. Your work will be critical in calculating and analyzing all external loads on the aircraft, including those from manoeuvres, turbulence, landing impacts, ground-handling etc. Ensuring that the aircraft remains free of flutter and other aeroelastic phenomena across its entire operating envelope through detailed modelling and analysis of aircraft structural dynamics. Deliver aircraft loads and aeroelastics documentation and data essential for the safe dimensioning of the aircraft structure and for achieving certification in accordance with time, cost, and quality standards. Provide recommendations for aircraft design, development processes, new technologies, and in-service operations, all related to the loads and aeroelastics field. Apply all relevant quality assurance processes, such as lessons learned, continuous improvement, and adherence to Quality Gates.
