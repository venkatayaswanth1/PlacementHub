Job Description

The Ola Group is a dynamic conglomerate comprising ANI Technologies, Ola Electric, and Krutrim, each contributing to our mission of redefining mobility, advancing sustainable transportation, and pioneering AI innovation. At ANI Technologies, we operate Ola Cabs, India's leading ride-hailing platform, serving over 250 million customers across 3 continents, offering employees a fast-paced, tech-driven environment where innovation thrives. Ola Electric is at the forefront of the electric vehicle revolution, with our state-of-the-art Futurefactory in Tamil Nadu, producing over 5 million electric two-wheelers annually and creating thousands of jobs. Krutrim, our AI arm, is dedicated to developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence models and infrastructure, empowering India to lead in the digital age. Job Summary: In this role, you will work alongside senior engineers across Cloud Infrastructure, Automation, and Android, contributing to platforms that power millions of users and large-scale distributed systems. This is a learning-intensive program designed to groom you into a strong Cloud, Automation, or Android engineer. You will gain hands-on exposure to modern tools, large-scale architectures, and real-world engineering challenges at Ola-scale. Key Result Areas: Develop & Maintain robust mobile and cloud-integrated features across iOS/Android platforms. Automate Workflows using API automation, mobile automation, and data-driven testing to improve release quality. Integrate Cloud Services by working with backend, Kafka streams, databases (SQL/MySQL), and internal APIs. Debug & Troubleshoot app, API, and infrastructure-level issues with strong analytical and data skills. Collaborate Cross-Functionally with product, design, backend, and QA teams to deliver high-performance apps. Adopt AI Tools to accelerate development, testing, and reliability across the engineering workflow. Competency Requirements: B.Tech/M.Tech in Computer Science, IT, Software Engineering, or related fields. Strong grasp of CS fundamentals (OOP, DS, Algo). Basic proficiency in Swift/Kotlin/Java for mobile development. Hands-on familiarity with Appium, API testing, Postman, and automation workflows. Understanding of REST APIs, SQL/MySQL, Kafka, and cloud-integrated systems. Ability to debug logs, crashes, performance issues, and network calls efficiently. Working proficiency with Git and collaborative version-control practices. Comfort using AI tools for faster coding, debugging, and documentation. Perks of working with Ola: Build technology that impacts millions at massive scale. Work with top engineers solving complex, high-performance problems. Exposure to cutting-edge systems across mobility, EV, and AI businesses. High ownership and visibility with leadership. Fast-track career growth in a high-velocity engineering environment.
