Job Description

Profile: Analyst We are looking for individuals who want to make a career in Analytics, Data Science & New Age Technology. If you are passionate about Analytics and want to make an impact by challenging yourself, this is what the role entails: • Data & Analytics o Collect, clean, and integrate commercial and healthcare data (e.g., sales, prescription, claims, patient-level). o Conduct descriptive and exploratory analyses to identify key patterns and trends o Develop dashboards and visualizations using BI tools (Tableau, Power BI). • Advanced Analytics & Data Science o Apply statistical methods and machine learning techniques (regression, clustering, forecasting, classification). o Support variety of business problems across forecasting, customer segmentation, marketing mix, patient analytics domains using advanced analytics. o Develop ML models using state-of-the-art classical algorithms and Deep Learning approaches. o Build and maintain GenAI / Agentic AI applications. • ML Ops & Engineering o Support in ML Engineering / MLOps: model deployment, monitoring, retraining pipelines, and CI/CD practices. o Work with modern tech stacks (Python, PySpark, Databricks, SQL, TensorFlow/PyTorch, cloud platforms). o Apply Agile methodologies to deliver iterative and high-quality analytics solutions. o Contribute to automation, process improvement, documentation, and building reusable knowledge assets o Contribute to SDLC processes including design reviews, code reviews, deployment planning, and testing • Business Insights & Delivery o Translate analytical outputs into clear business implications for clients. o Work closely with onshore teams and product managers for enhancements and client-ready insights & recommendations o Participate in requirements gathering, propose technology/modeling solutions, and contribute to development within agreed timelines o Business consulting on sales consulting and operation areas like territory design, call plan / dynamic targeting design, Incentive compensation design etc. What We Offer • A clear career progression path with opportunities to learn industry best practices and gain exposure to global life sciences operations, which will enable growth towards data analyst, operations lead, or client engagement roles. • Opportunity to work on impactful analytics and data science projects for leading global pharma clients. • Structured training across data analytics, data science, and pharma domain knowledge. • Mentorship and career development opportunities within a collaborative environment. • Exposure to AI/GenAI-enabled delivery models, shaping the future of healthcare analytics.
