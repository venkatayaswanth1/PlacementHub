Job Description

Dezerv is a house of investing solutions for high net worth and affluent Indians. Dezerv is co-founded by Sandeep Jethwani, Vaibhav Porwal, and Sahil Contractor. They have led and founded successful wealth management businesses and managed over USD 7 billion in assets. The Dezerv team brings together decades of investing expertise from leading global financial institutions like JP Morgan, UBS, BNP Paribas, etc. Our team of experts monitor the performance of portfolios and rebalance them if required to ensure long-term success. We are backed by marque firms like Premji Invest, Accel, Elevation, Matrix, etc. Since inception, our clients have trusted us with over 13000+ crs of their assets. Why are we building Dezerv? Investing is stressful and emotional. Building & growing wealth is difficult and time-consuming. Most individuals struggle with managing their investments and money. Our goal is to help individuals grow their wealth without the stress, time, and costs involved in a traditional investment. At Dezerv, We are building a platform that leverages our decades of investment expertise to help individuals invest better for their future. What are we trying to solve/mission? We are passionate about helping Indians invest better. We manage investments with active oversight to help both, sophisticated and new investors build long term wealth across various market conditions. What are you expected to do? ● Work as part of a team developing applications and services using Agile development methods ● Build microservices by writing reusable, testable, and efficient code ● Effectively use tools and ingenuity to identify and fix defects before they become a problem ● Develop and implement application architecture that is modular and scalable after understanding the business and design requirements ● Help come up with estimates for project and individual tasks ● Help the team in solving technical challenges ● Have a good understanding on how applications will be deployed, managed and operated in the production environment ● Make sure coding standards are followed through detailed code reviews ● Make sure that the code is well commented and documented, along with any TODOs on technical debt and shortcuts that need to be addressed later What are we looking for? ● In-depth knowledge of Golang, Java, or any other language that supports multithreading ● Excellent knowledge of MongoDB ● Ability to create database schemas that represent and support business processes ● Strong analytical skills and problem-solving aptitude ● Ability to take project and product ownership for successful delivery within the decided budgets and timelines ● Should understand fundamental design patterns and principles behind a scalable and secure application ● Should have strong communication, decision-making skills ● Knowledge of frontend technologies like React and Redux will be an added plus
