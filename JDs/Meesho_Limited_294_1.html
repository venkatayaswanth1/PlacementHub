We place special emphasis on the continuous growth of each team member - and we do this with regular 1-1s and open communication. AI-first Approach: At Meesho, we are building for the future with AI at our core. If you're excited by the intersection of cutting-edge AI and real-world impact at Bharat scale, Meesho is the place for you!
