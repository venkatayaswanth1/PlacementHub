Job Description

As a Voice AI Engineer I, you will help build the next generation of voice-driven automation agents that power GENIE’s conversational interface. You’ll work on speech recognition, synthesis, intent detection, and multimodal understanding, integrating these capabilities with enterprise-grade systems to create seamless, human-like interactions. You’ll also work on developing multimodal interfaces that seamlessly integrate voice, text, and visuals to deliver natural, conversational user experiences. Responsibilities: ● Design and develop real-time speech-to-text (ASR) and text-to-speech (TTS) pipelines optimized for low latency and high accuracy ● Work on end-to-end voice agent frameworks, integrating speech models with LLMs for natural conversations ● Build and fine-tune intent classification and dialogue management systems ● Implement voice biometrics, emotion detection, and multilingual support for enterprise voice agents ● Collaborate with AI and backend engineers to deploy scalable voice solutions across industries ● Collaborate with frontend teams to design and implement intuitive voice + UI experiences, ensuring smooth transitions between spoken and visual interactions ● Research and prototype cutting-edge models in speech enhancement, prompt-based voice synthesis, and audio understanding Requirements: ● B.Tech / B.S. / Dual degree in CSE, M&C, EE, AI or equivalent ● Proficiency in Python and its libraries like PyTorch, TensorFlow or Transformers ● Understanding of speech processing concepts such as ASR, TTS, NLU, NLP and signal processing fundamentals ● Experience or coursework in audio feature extraction, acoustic modelling, or language modelling ● Exposure to speech APIs (OpenAI Whisper, Coqui, Deepgram, etc.) or LLM-integrated voice frameworks is a plus
