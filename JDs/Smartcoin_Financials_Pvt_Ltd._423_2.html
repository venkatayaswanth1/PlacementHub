Job Description

Responsibilities: 1. Credit Risk & Underwriting: - Analyze borrower data to develop credit scoring models and risk assessment frameworks. - Monitor and optimize loan approval algorithms to minimize defaults. - Design predictive models for early warning signals and delinquency detection. 2. Collections & Recovery: - Build models to identify high-risk accounts and prioritize collections efforts. - Analyze repayment patterns to optimize recovery strategies. - Generate dashboards for collections teams to track performance metrics. 3. Customer Analytics & Personalization: - Segment users based on behavior, creditworthiness, and transaction patterns. - Design data-driven strategies to improve customer engagement and retention. - Support marketing campaigns with insights on cross-selling and upselling opportunities. 4. Fraud Detection & Compliance: - Analyze transactional data to detect anomalies, suspicious activities, and potential fraud. - Work on building machine learning models for real-time fraud prevention. - Collaborate with compliance teams to ensure regulatory requirements are met. 5. Business & Product Analytics: - Track KPIs, loan portfolio performance, and operational efficiency metrics. - Conduct ad-hoc analysis to support product decisions, pricing, and strategy. - Build dashboards for executives and stakeholders for data-driven decision making. Skills Required: - Strong proficiency in Python / R for data analysis and modeling. - Expertise in SQL and working with large structured/unstructured datasets. - Knowledge of statistical modeling, machine learning, and predictive analytics. - Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or Looker). - Understanding of lending products, credit risk, and financial metrics. - Strong problem-solving, analytical, and communication skills. - Familiarity with A/B testing, experimentation, and hypothesis-driven analysis.
