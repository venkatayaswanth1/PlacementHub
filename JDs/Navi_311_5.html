Job Description

Business Analyst - I Location: Bangalore About the Team The Analytics team at Navi plays a pivotal role in driving data-informed decision-making across the organization. We work closely with all business functions to generate insights, build visibility, and validate hypotheses that shape strategy and execution. Our work spans key verticals: Business and Growth Analytics, Credit & Credit Risk Analytics, Collections & Biz-fin Analytics, Self-Serve and Automation. About the Role As a Business Analyst, you will be at the intersection of data, business, and technology—working closely with Operations, Product, and Program leaders to transform raw data into meaningful insights that drive strategic decisions. By analyzing large datasets, identifying trends, and uncovering root causes, you will generate insights that improve processes, enhance customer experience, and drive growth. The role goes beyond preparing reports—you will work directly with business leaders to answer key questions, evaluate opportunities, and recommend actions that deliver measurable impact. With strong problem-solving skills, business acumen, and data-driven thinking, you will help the organization make smarter, faster, and more effective decisions. What We Expect From You Data Collection, Validation & Analysis – Uncovering trends and correlations through mining and analysis to develop insights, and analyze trends, patterns, and root causes to generate actionable insights. Reporting, Visualization & GenAI Applications – Develop and maintain standardized and ad-hoc reports, dashboards, and visualizations. Leverage GenAI-powered tools to simplify complex analyses, accelerate reporting, and present data in intuitive formats for faster decision-making. Business Consulting & Collaboration – Partner with Product, Ops, Finance, Compliance, and Engineering teams to bridge business goals with customer needs, provide data-backed recommendations, and influence strategic initiatives. Process Improvement & Automation – Continuously identify opportunities to streamline, simplify, and automate redundant processes, enhancing efficiency and scalability in a dynamic fintech environment. Data Governance & Compliance – Ensure adherence to data governance policies and regulatory requirements while handling sensitive information, maintaining accuracy, consistency, and security in reporting. Must Haves Analytical Problem-Solving – Ability to break down complex business problems into structured, solvable components and identify the right approach to address them. Statistical Foundations – Conceptual understanding of basic statistical concepts such as sampling, distributions, measures of central tendency, and hypothesis testing. Business Acumen – Awareness of fundamental business concepts like revenue, cost, margin, and funnel metrics, with the ability to link data insights to business impact. Communication & Collaboration – Strong written and verbal communication skills to present insights clearly and work effectively with cross-functional teams. (Good to Have) Data Handling Skills – Comfort with identifying, extracting, and validating relevant data using tools like SQL and Excel to solve business problems and test hypotheses.
