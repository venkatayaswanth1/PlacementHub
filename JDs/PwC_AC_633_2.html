Job Description

About PwC Join PwC AC India and play a vital role in supporting our Advisory, Assurance, Tax and Business Services teams. Gain professional growth and immersive learning opportunities as you deliver value through virtual client engagement worldwide. Shape your future as a member of our diverse global talent network. As an Associate, you’ll work as part of a team of problem solvers with extensive consulting and industry experience, helping our clients solve their complex business issues from strategy to execution. What does this role entail? Our Operations & Supply Chain Solutions team works with our global clients to design and implement growth, operational and customer focused strategies for sustainable competitive advantage. Our thought leadership and unparalleled experience help clients turn formidable challenges into market advantage across the value chain and around the globe. Additionally, our extensive expertise in various industries allow us to serve clients with consulting services with a focus on the most profitable elements of the value chain to create scalable businesses that will deliver increased sustainable profits. Our Management Consultants work with the client and project teams to support global engagements from India through activities that are driven towards delivering results - conduct analyses and develop insights, prepare excel models, analyze large sets of data, capture as-is processes, prepare work plans, design to-be processes. In addition, consultants also support project lead, work directly with client teams and facilitate meetings to enable decision making, organize and prepare recommendations on client issues, and participate actively in new business development, thought leadership and firm building activities. Core responsibilities as an Associate would entail (but not limited to) As a team member, you’ll work as part of a team of problem solvers with extensive consulting and industry experience, helping our clients solve their complex business issues from strategy to execution. Specific areas of focus include but are not limited to: • Supply Chain: As a part of our supply chain team, you will be involved in conducting supply chain diagnostics and assessments, assisting in supply chain cost and performance benchmarking, and participating in process design and reverse supply chain activities. Your role will also include supporting service operations such as parts management, warehousing, and distributor/retailer management. • Planning: In the planning domain, you will assist in creating sales forecasts and demand planning. Your contributions will extend to sales and operations planning, inventory planning, supply planning, capacity planning, production planning, and material requirements planning, ensuring the seamless flow of operations. • Manufacturing & Digital Operations: Your role in manufacturing and digital operations will include performing manufacturing footprint analysis and make-vs-buy analysis, supporting production scheduling, and applying lean, six sigma, and kaizen methodologies for manufacturing process optimization. You will be involved in cutting-edge concepts and technologies such as Smart Factory, Connected Supply Chain, Industry 4.0, and Digital Product Development. Additionally, you will assist in implementing Quality Management System (QMS) processes and technologies, participating in quality control/assurance, quality process validation, and inspection management. • Sourcing and Procurement: In sourcing and procurement, you will conduct spend analysis and category savings assessments, assist in procurement diagnostics and process design, and support end-to-end procurement transformation and modernization efforts. Your responsibilities will include participating in the design and execution of strategic sourcing initiatives, such as RFI/RFP design and analysis, and assisting in supplier management and contract management activities. • Source to Pay Transformations: Working with Procure-to-Pay (P2P) platforms like Coupa, you will serve as an implementer and administrator, assisting in spend and commodity taxonomies, requisitioning, PO generation, receiving and invoice processing, and e-Invoicing submittals. • Logistics / Warehousing: Your role in logistics and warehousing will involve assisting in developing logistics strategies and identifying cost reduction opportunities for logistics, transportation, and warehouse operations. You will support logistics network design and optimization and participate in inbound and outbound logistics and warehouse functional activities. • Product Innovation: In product innovation, you will contribute to product development and lifecycle management, assist in process design and mapping, and support market/trend assessments and insource vs. outsource analysis.
