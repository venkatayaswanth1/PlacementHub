Job Description

As an Agentic AI Engineer I, you will be part of the team building next-generation autonomous agents that enable enterprises to automate decisions, conversations, and tasks with minimal human input. You’ll design and train systems that can reason, act, and adapt integrating LLMs with real-world tools, APIs, and structured data. Responsibilities: ● Design and develop agentic AI pipelines capable of performing multi-step reasoning and task orchestration ● Build LLM-integrated applications that interact with APIs, databases, and enterprise tools ● Research and implement cutting-edge advancements in autonomous agents, function calling, and reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF) ● Contribute to GENIE’s knowledge graph and context memory systems for adaptive decision-making ● Optimize agent efficiency through prompt engineering, caching and behavioural tuning ● Work with cross-functional teams to integrate agentic solutions into enterprise-grade SaaS environments Requirements: ● B.Tech / B.S. / Dual degree in CSE, M&C, EE, AI or equivalent ● Strong programming foundation in Python and exposure to frameworks like LangChain, Llama Index, or OpenAI SDKs ● Familiarity with transformer architectures, prompt design, and LLM fine-tuning ● Hands-on experience with APIs, REST frameworks, or microservices is a plus ● Passion for autonomous systems, generative AI, and agentic architectures ● Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, research-driven environment where innovation meets practical deployment
