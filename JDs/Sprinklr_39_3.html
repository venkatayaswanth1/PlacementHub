Job Description

The Product team at Sprinklr is one of the biggest drivers in ensuring product led growth of the company. Product team is responsible for end-to-end ownership of the several Sprinklr products. Unlike several companies focusing on 1 or 2 products, limiting the scope of exposure for a Product Manager; Sprinklr offers 30+ different products to work on - including but not limited to CCaaS, Chat bots, Marketing automation, Advertisement optimization, Social Engagement, Web chat, Communities and Forums, etc, both on web and mobile. All Sprinklr products are AI first, i.e. it will give you exposure and first hand experience with several AI technologies and techniques - NLP, NLU, computer vision, NLG etc. As an PA you will work on For the first 12 months the role will be heavily focused on product knowledge and product delivery. This phase will give you complete exposure to the Sprinklr product and make you the Subject Matter Expert and go to person for several areas of Sprinklr product. ● Working as a product analyst, talk to some of the largest brands out there, understand their business use cases and then configure the Sprinklr product for them to ensure they are able to solve their business problems in an optimal manner using Sprinklr product ● Own live projects with clients and ensure timely delivery working in a cross functional team of Project managers, Implementation consultants, Product Managers. ● Be a single point of contact from the Product team in respective cross functional client engagement teams. Post 12 months, you will switch to owning client engagements from the product side where you will… ● Own a few large client accounts to ensure their continued success in using Sprinklr products. You will be responsible for all product delivery, adoption and success for these clients. ● Work as a product consultant, talk to some of the largest brands out there, understand their business use cases and then help standardize and streamline the Sprinklr product and it’s delivery to ensure we are able to solve their business problems in an optimal manner using Sprinklr product ● Constant learning and collaborating with the team to develop best practices on product configuration with direct exposure in working with biggest consumer, business & technology brands across the globe. ● Work as a team to understand product adoption by various brands and establish standard practices to improve adoption After this, depending on the performance you can switch to product development side of the product management role.
