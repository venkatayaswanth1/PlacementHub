Job Description

About the role As an APM at Blinkit, you’ll be part of the team that powers India’s fastest-growing quick commerce ecosystem. If you love solving problems, enjoy working with data, and want to make real impact early in your career, this role is for you. What you’ll get to do Take ownership of high-impact projects that shape our operations and customer experience. Use data (Excel + basic SQL) to uncover insights and drive decisions. Work with cross-functional teams—Category, Supply, Ops, Product—to bring ideas to life. Spend time on the ground to understand how the business truly runs. Be part of an environment where learning is fast, challenges are real, and your work is visible. What we’re looking for Strong analytical fundamentals with hands-on Excel and basic SQL skills. A problem-solver who asks “why?” and goes deep into numbers. Someone who can hustle, adapt quickly, and deliver at pace. A mix of confidence and humility—comfortable working with senior leaders and on-ground teams alike. High ownership, curiosity, and willingness to explore new ideas. Why Blinkit? Work in one of the fastest-growing consumer businesses in India. Steep learning curve, real responsibility from Day 1, and exposure to business-critical functions. A culture that values long-term thinking, meritocracy, and people who take initiative. An opportunity to grow with a company that’s constantly evolving—and letting you evolve with it.
