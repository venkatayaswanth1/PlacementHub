Learn more at https://www.burgersinghonline.com/ About the Role We're looking for sharp, driven individuals who are eager to learn and grow in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial QSR environment. As a Revenue Analyst, you will take end-to-end ownership of the P&L for multiple stores, work cross-functionally to drive initiatives, and be directly responsible for improving profitability. This role sits at the intersection of consulting and execution — perfect for someone who wants to understand how food businesses run and scale.
