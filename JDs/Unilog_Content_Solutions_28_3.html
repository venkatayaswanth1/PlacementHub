Designation: -Trainee Software Engineer Location: Bangalore/Mysore/Remote Job Type: Full-time Department: Software R&D About the Role We are looking for a motivated AI/ML Trainee Software Engineer who is passionate about applying modern AI techniques to solve real-world problems. This role provides the opportunity to work with Generative AI, LLMs, and MCP, ACP, and AI Agent frameworks, contributing to next-generation AI-driven solutions within our CTO Office. Key Responsibilities 1. Assist in designing, training, and deploying ML and DL models under mentorship. 2. Work on projects involving MCP, ACP, and AI Agent creation. 3. Support development of intelligent search and RAG workflows using vector embedding. 4. Collaborate with senior engineers to build prototypes and POCs. 5. Perform data preprocessing, experimentation, and documentation of results. 6. Learn and adopt new AI/ML frameworks and research advancements. Required Qualifications 1. B.Tech or M.Tech in Computer Science, Data Science, or related field from IIT. 2. Strong understanding of ML fundamentals and programming in Python. 3. Familiarity with ML frameworks such as PyTorch or Tensor Flow. 4. Good analytical and problem-solving skills with willingness to learn. Preferred Qualifications 1. Academic project or internship experience involving MCP, ACP, and Agent creation. 2. Academic Project experience with LLMs, LRMs, and Generative AI. 3. Exposure to vector databases such as Pinecone, FAISS, Weaviate, or PG Vector. 4.
