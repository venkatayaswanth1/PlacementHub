About Us: NoBroker.com is the world's largest brokerage-free real estate marketplace, dedicated to transforming the real estate experience with cutting-edge products. Our comprehensive real estate ecosystem includes NoBrokerHood for community management, NoBroker HomeServices for property management, and a range of services like Legal Services, Rental Agreements, Packers & Movers, HomeLoans, Rent Payments, and more. ConvoZen.AI: We are also proud to offer ConvoZen.AI, our state-of-the-art conversational AI tool. ConvoZen.AI leverages advanced NLP and large language model (LLM) technologies to provide organizations with actionable insights from customer conversations. This innovative solution is revolutionizing how businesses interact with their customers, making it a focal point of our data science endeavors. Role: As an Associate Data Scientist at NoBroker.com, you will play a crucial role in advancing ConvoZen.AI. You'll work with a dedicated team to develop and enhance our conversational AI capabilities, applying NLP, LLMs, and other machine learning techniques to create impactful solutions. This role offers the opportunity to work with modern tools and technologies. Job Description- 1) You will be working closely with the Data Science & Machine Learning Team solving problems involving large sets of data. The problems will be applied and oriented towards shipping for production. 2) You will work on large sets of data in distributed large scale systems. 3) Take part in validation, monitoring, reporting maintenance , deployment and upgradation of existing ML models & heuristic algorithms in production. 4) You will require to demo & communicate results clearly and effectively to the management. 5) You will be working with versatile sets of data including tabular, images, text, geospatial & audio. 6) You will code logics and algorithms efficiently and fast with great readability, reusability, and modularity, that will be going into production directly impacting millions of our users. 7) Ensure the implementation and adherence to robust IT security practices, including but not limited to network and system security, data protection, encryption standards, vulnerability assessments, and compliance with industry regulations, to safeguard the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of organizational assets Qualification -Engineering background with adept mathematical & programming experience in C++ & Python -Relevant Projects/Internship/OpenSource contributions in the field is a must -Versatile problem solver, equipped with strong analytical and quantitative skills suited to approach various kinds of challenges in complex environments -Self Starter with a strong technical orientation -Strong coding skills and hands-on data experience -Understanding of Machine Learning basics both classical and deep. -Basic understanding of NLP, Vision & Speech problems in Machine What do we have to offer? Be part of a start-up from the very beginning, work directly with founders, lead your area of expertise, and be a part of this exciting growth journey of changing the world.
