Job Description

Business Analyst : As a Business Analyst, you will be at the heart of innovation, engineering intelligence into real-world solutions that shape the future of businesses across industries. ● A Natural Translator of Business to Tech: You can listen to a client’s complex business problem and seamlessly translate it into clear, actionable technical requirements. You connect business context with data and delivery. ● A Precision-Focused Organizer: You thrive in structured chaos—drafting BRDs, MoMs, and weekly updates comes naturally to you. You ensure no insight or action point is lost between meetings, sprints, or reports. ● A Strategic Collaborator: Whether working with architects to shape solutions or aligning with delivery leads to assign resources, you operate at the intersection of teams—making sure each one is moving toward the same outcome. ● A Confident Communicator: From leading sprint demos to interacting with global clients across onsite and offshore projects, you’re clear, persuasive, and always aligned with the “why” behind the “what.” ● A Curious Analyst: You don’t stop at the obvious—you explore raw data to spot trends, anomalies, and opportunities, and you translate those into insights that truly move the business forward. ● A Long-Term Thinker with a Bias for Action: You’re not only focused on solving the problem today—you can envision the long-term impact of the solution and ensure it’s executed with excellence. Business Analyst Sales Engineer : As a Business Analyst Sales Engineer, you will be on the front lines, working with a highly energetic team to shape the future of our clients. ● Become a Strategist: Partner with our technical and sales experts to map the competitive landscape for our clients, identifying their most pressing needs and charting the course for how Quantiphi's solutions can lead their transformation. ● Excel at Solution Definition: Deconstruct complex business challenges, methodically capture requirements, and help scope the innovative Data, AI solutions, leveraging the power of Cloud that form the foundation of our client proposals. ● Architect a Clear Vision: Craft and deliver compelling presentations that outline a clear vision for a client's technological transformation, demonstrating how our solutions can reshape their entire business. ● Build the Business Case: Weave powerful narratives using Quantiphi's incredible success stories to build trust and prove why we are the world's leading AI-first digital engineering partner.
