Job Description

Role: Associate Research Strategist Location: Mumbai Type: Full Time On-site Are you passionate about understanding what drives consumer behavior? Do you thrive on turning complex data into compelling stories that shape business strategy? MavenMagnet AI is looking for a Research Strategists to join our dynamic team and help global brands make smarter, insight-driven decisions. As a Research Strategist, you’ll work on end-to-end qualitative research projects for some of the world’s most recognized brands across industries like CPG, Automotive, Tech and Telecom and Hospitality. You’ll harness the power of Conversation Research - our cutting-edge, AI-powered methodology - to uncover deep, actionable insights from digital conversations. You’ll also collaborate closely with our data science team to co-develop Generative AI-powered insight solutions, pushing the boundaries of how qualitative research is conducted and delivered. What you will do? • Undertake qualitative research initiatives from design to delivery, using innovative conversation research methodologies • Analyze digital conversations and cultural trends to extract meaningful consumer insights • Collaborate with data scientists to develop and refine Gen AI-powered insight tools and frameworks • Deliver strategic recommendations through compelling storytelling and data synthesis • Work cross-functionally with product, marketing, and sales teams to embed insights into business strategy • Stay ahead of the curve by exploring emerging research techniques and industry trends What we are looking for? • Hands-on experience in qualitative market research or consumer insights • Master’s degree in Marketing, Psychology, Sociology, or Social Anthropology • Proven expertise in research design, content analysis, and insight presentation • Strong command of English and Hindi (written and verbal) • Analytical mindset with a knack for interpreting complex qualitative data • Excellent communication and storytelling skills • A portfolio that showcases your ability to turn research into impact Why join us? • A fast-paced, intellectually stimulating environment where high performers thrive • Competitive salary • Work with elite professionals and global clients • Opportunities to shape the future of insights through AI-driven innovation • A culture that values curiosity, ownership, and bold thinking About the Company: MavenMagnet AI is transforming the insights generation industry by delivering in depth analytics with significant time and cost efficiency as compared to traditional alternatives. Our SaaS platform - Insights Assistant - uses disruptive deep learning analytical capabilities to harness innumerable datasets, and empower brands and organization with actionable qualitative insights on a quantitative scale to help them make more efficient business decisions. Focus area is conversational research. Conversational research does not involve moderation of discussions, surveys or online panels. Instead, it uses the conversations that the consumers are having on various online interactive platforms with their friends and family to gather true insights. It is observation without disruption. It is free of bias or "staging" that are inherently part of other traditional research methodologies. While the starting point is exploratory research in the purest form, the large sample size enables quantification of insights. Services: Digital ethnography, category research, brand imagery mapping, integrated campaign tracking and optimization, corporate reputation analysis etc. Video Link: https://vimeo.com/868602564 If you're ready to bring your passion and dedication to a team that’s redefining the future of consumer insights, we’d love to hear from you.
