Job Description

Responsibilities:- Data Analysis & Reporting: Collect, analyze, and interpret data using SQL / Python Perform in-depth data analysis to identify areas of improvement and optimization within the e-commerce site and digital marketing efforts. Business Process Optimization: Identify inefficiencies and recommend solutions to improve operational workflows and business processes Collaborate with stakeholders to define project scope, objectives, and deliverables related to e-commerce business enhancements. Continuously monitor and assess the effectiveness of implemented solutions and processes. E-commerce Strategy & Improvement: Analyze customer behavior, sales trends, and product performance to propose actionable insights and strategies to optimize the online shopping experience. Recommend strategies for improving customer retention, cart conversion rates Participate in user testing, feedback collection, and A/B testing initiatives to enhance site functionality and customer satisfaction. Cross-Functional Collaboration: Work with product managers, developers and marketers to ensure that business requirements are met and customer experiences are optimized. Act as a liaison between technical teams and business teams to ensure alignment on objectives, timelines, and deliverables. Market Research: Conduct competitive analysis and industry research to understand emerging e-commerce trends and best practices. Stay informed on new technologies, tools, and methodologies that can enhance business operations. Project Management Support: Support the planning and execution of projects related to e-commerce development and business optimization. Track project timelines, milestones, and deliverables, ensuring that projects are delivered on time and within scope.
