Job Description

Job Description – Upstream Position: Scientist for Upstream (Cell Culture) Role Summary We are looking for highly motivated and enthusiastic fresh postgraduates from Chemical Engineering, Biochemical Engineering, Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Life sciences or related disciplines to join our Upstream Process Development team. This is an excellent opportunity for entry-level talent to gain hands-on experience in biologics development, participate in cross-functional projects, and contribute to the design and scale-up of purification processes from lab to manufacturing scale in a dynamic, collaborative environment. Key Responsibilities: Individually contributing to the design, execution, analysis, documentation and archival of complex experiments related to upstream process development of biologics at lab, pilot and manufacturing scale. • Planning and execution of experiments to support process development, scale up, process characterization etc. • Execution of experiments in shake flasks and bioreactors. Coordination and support pilot scale batches • Documentation • Handling cell culture related equipment and aseptic operations • Prepare technical reports, presentations etc. • Supporting technology transfer from R&D • Participating actively in cross-functional meetings and communicate key findings. • Maintaining a safe workplace by adhering to prevalent EHS and OSHA standards. • Collaborate with QA, QC, Manufacturing and Regulatory functions in order to successfully deliver drugs to customers. • Contributing to project planning, logistics. • Providing support to other team members as per priority and requirement. • Perform literature reviews and summarize relevant topics for the development of work stream. Key Skillset • Minimum of M. Tech/M.Sc. in Chemical Engineering/Biochemical Engineering/ Biotechnology/Biochemistry/Lie sciences and other related fields a must. • Knowledge of aseptic handling, related cell culture techniques and equipment. • Has basic knowledge of bioreactors, cell biology and biochemistry • Recording and reviewing of raw data in accordance with good documentation practices • Prepare work presentations, write technical documents (process protocols, technical/development reports), procedures (SOP’s/IOP’s/EOP’s) • Understands statistics and can perform data analysis. Enthusiastic to build on their statistical skills. • Adaptable to work in an agile, fast paced environment with competing priorities, evolving goals. • Able to work within a team and complement strengths of other team members • Able to work independently, possess excellent communication skill, problem-solving skills. • Able to work with cross-functional teams successfully and communicate technical findings.
