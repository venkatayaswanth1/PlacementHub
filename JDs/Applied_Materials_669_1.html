Job Description

About Applied Applied Materials is the leader in materials engineering solutions. Virtually every new chip produced and every advanced display in the world has been processed one of our solutions. Our expertise in modifying materials at atomic levels and on an industrial scale enables customers to transform possibilities into reality. At Applied Materials, our innovations make possible technology shaping the future. To achieve this, we employ some of the best, brightest, and most talented people in the world who work together as part of a winning team. While virtually every nationality, culture, and background are currently represented within Applied Materials, we strive for a more robust Culture of Inclusion (COI) and diversity. Our Team You will be part of the Global Quality group that ensures product quality & reliability goals are met throughout the product life cycle. Your day-to-day work will involve intense interactions with highly accomplished architects, engineers, and product specialists who are passionate about path breaking architectures and creating new solutions to bring in greater ROI for our customers. If you are a person who is self-motivated and passionate and likes to lead from the front, have multi-disciplinary skills to critically review design solutions, provide recommendations and solutions to mitigate them towards creating technology products, then this will be the right opportunity for you. Your Opportunity You will be part of a highly motivated team designing a complex wafer processing tool and be responsible for ensuring product quality & reliability that meets the program and customer expectations. You will be collaborating with experts from diverse backgrounds which will expand your horizon and bring in growth in responsibility and career. You will be constantly pushing the limits of the technology and will be growing as an expert in a functional area. Key Responsibilities • Develops and executes product qualification plans • Apply Quality & Reliability tools (i.e., SPC concept of process stability and capability, Reliability Apportionment, FMECA, 8D) • Supports customers by driving quality improvement programs • Perform or requests failure analysis of components under test. • Manages the new product development qualification requirements • Assist Engineering in the selection of more reliable components and in using reliable design practices through participation in formal design reviews and engineering meetings. • Prepares reliability status presentations and reports to project teams and senior management. • Utilizes statistical techniques and testing to identify and prevent reliability problems • Develops, applies, revises, maintains and/ or tests reliability standards to ensure these align with customer expectations • Implement methods and procedures for inspecting, testing and evaluating the reliability of products • Demonstrates knowledge of Accelerated Life Test, Life Distribution (Weibull), Statistical Sampling, Hypothesis Testing, Reliability Modeling, Stress Tests (HALT)
