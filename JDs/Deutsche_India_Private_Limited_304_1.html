Job Description

About the role: As a graduate in Deutsche Bank, you will have the opportunity to work with teams supporting the Bank’s Investment Banking business globally and learn about banking from different viewpoints, which will give you a career advantage. As a graduate, your work may include: – Connecting and working with global teams within the Investment Bank, and interacting with bankers, traders, and structurers in different regions across the world – Developing a strong grasp of ongoing macro events and their impact on various asset classes – Undertaking in-depth analysis of companies/sectors/transactions – due diligence and financial modelling of companies, industry analysis, quantitative and technical analysis, and benchmarking or supporting in the execution of deals and flow trades – Developing a holistic view of the business including the DB product suite, its operational markets, applicable regulations and on the back of that, create marketing content based on deal pitches, new trades done, DB capability expansion, regulatory changes, etc Areas in which you can work: Origination & Advisory: – Industry Coverage (For e.g. Industrials, Healthcare, TMT, etc.) – Product Coverage (For e.g. M&A, Ratings Advisory, Equity & Debt Capital Markets, etc.) What we look for: We recruit graduates who are intelligent and adaptable with excellent interpersonal skills. They have degrees in many different disciplines. Even though good levels of numeracy are important for some roles, you won’t necessarily need a formal mathematical or financial background. We have very comprehensive training programmes that will give you all the technical knowledge you’ll need to build a career with us. To be eligible for the 2026 Graduate Programme you must: • Complete your studies (all final exams, coursework and have no outstanding requirements for graduation) by July 2026. • Be able to start full time work in July 2026, subject to local working legislation and visa requirements
