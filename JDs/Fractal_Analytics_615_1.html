Role: AI Engineer About the Company: Fractal is one of the most prominent providers of Artificial Intelligence to Fortune 500® companies. Fractal's vision is to power every human decision in the enterprise, and bring AI, engineering, and design to help the world's most admired companies. Fractal's businesses include Crux Intelligence (AI driven business intelligence), Eugenie.ai (AI for sustainability), Asper.ai (AI for revenue growth management) and Senseforth.ai (conversational AI for sales and customer service). Fractal incubated Qure.ai, a leading player in healthcare AI for detecting Tuberculosis and Lung cancer. Fractal currently has 5500+ employees across 16 global locations, including the United States, UK, Ukraine, India, Singapore, and Australia. Fractal has been recognized as 'Great Workplace' and 'India's Best Workplaces for Women' in the top 100 (large) category by The Great Place to Work® Institute; featured as a leader in Customer Analytics Service Providers Wave™ 2021, Computer Vision Consultancies Wave™ 2020 & Specialized Insights Service Providers Wave™ 2020 by Forrester Research Inc., a leader in Analytics & AI Services Specialists Peak Matrix 2022 by Everest Group and recognized as an 'Honorable Vendor' in 2022 Magic Quadrant™ for data & analytics by Gartner Inc. For more information, visit fractal.ai About The Team At Fractal, engineers do not just write code, they build intelligence. They design systems that learn, reason, and act; systems that enable the next wave of Agentic and Generative AI for global enterprises. As an Engineer in our AI Platforms and Solutions team, you will build core AI infrastructure, model orchestration layers, and agent frameworks that power Fractal’s decision-intelligence ecosystem. Your work will go beyond products you will help create an ecosystem of selflearning agents, scalable data systems, and intelligent experiences that reshape how businesses think, decide, and act. You’ll collaborate with Fractal’s AI researchers, product engineers, and FDE teams to translate cutting-edge AI research into production-grade platforms such as our flagship Cogentiq. You will work on systems that operationalize reasoning, autonomy, and intelligence at scale, impacting millions of lives indirectly through our clients. What You Will Do • Design, develop, test, and scale core AI components, services, and infrastructure forming the backbone of Fractal’s platforms and agent ecosystems. • Engineer integrations between large language models (LLMs), reasoning engines, and decision systems. • Implement scalable microservices for agentic orchestration, context management, and autonomous task execution. • Collaborate across AI research, product, platform, and FDE teams to move from AI concept to enterprise deployment, translating papers to production. • Contribute to AI platform innovation, including observability, multi-agent coordination, memory architectures, and AI workflow automation. • Participate in AI innovation sprints and hackathons to build prototypes around reasoning, tool use, and autonomous collaboration between agents. • Review and optimize code, focusing on performance, scalability, and maintainability in high-load distributed environments. What You Bring • Strong programming skills in one or more languages : Python, C++, Java, or Go with a focus on clean, efficient, and scalable code. • Deep understanding of data structures, algorithms, OS fundamentals, and distributed systems. • Demonstrated curiosity about AI/ML, Generative AI, and agentic system design • Strong foundation in machine learning, LLMs, deep learning architectures, reinforcement learning, or NLP pipelines. • Experience with cloud-native AI systems (GCP, AWS, Azure), container orchestration (Kubernetes), and distributed compute frameworks (Ray, Spark, etc.). • Familiarity with LLM fine tuning, Reasoning model tuning, vector databases, embeddings, RAG systems, and multi-agent orchestration. • Passion for solving open-ended AI engineering problems: reasoning, planning, and human-machine collaboration. • Ability to join full-time in 2025.
