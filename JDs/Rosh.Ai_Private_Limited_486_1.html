Job Description

As an AI Engineer, you will work closely with RoshAi’s Applied AI and Perception teams on real-world autonomy problems, including computer vision, sensor fusion, reinforcement learning, and end-to-end driving models. You’ll contribute to data-driven R&D efforts that shape the next generation of intelligent vehicle systems. Key Responsibilities • Assist in building and training deep learning models for perception, planning, and control. • Develop and test algorithms for object detection, tracking, lane perception, and decision-making. • Work with large-scale multi-sensor datasets (camera, LiDAR, radar) for data curation and model validation. • Implement and optimize neural network architectures for real-time inference on embedded hardware. • Collaborate with cross-functional teams on simulation testing, data annotation, and model evaluation. • Document experiments, results, and insights to support continuous model improvement. Desired Skills • Strong fundamentals in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision. • Hands-on experience with Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, OpenCV, or ROS. • Familiarity with transformer models, reinforcement learning, or autonomous systems is a plus. • Good understanding of data preprocessing, augmentation, and performance optimization. • Excellent problem-solving and analytical thinking. • Ability to work in a fast-paced, research-driven environment. What you will gain: • Exposure to real-world autonomous driving projects and production-grade datasets. • Mentorship from industry experts with backgrounds in AI, automotive systems, and robotics. • Opportunity to publish papers, file patents, or continue with a Pre-Placement Offer (PPO) upon exceptional performance. • Experience in AI infrastructure, simulation environments, and applied R&D workflows.
