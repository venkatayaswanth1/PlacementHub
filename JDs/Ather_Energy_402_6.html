Job Description

This role is for an engineer who would take care of validating multiple requirements and constraints on various electro-mechanical systems for electric two wheelers and associated charging infrastructure BEFORE setting them as product specifications. ● Conceptualize electronic and electro-mechanical systems like dashboards, vehicle control unit, sensors, actuators and chargers for various applications. ● Translate product requirements into technical specifications for development by Engg teams. ● Research into existing and new technologies to understand feasibility of productizing them. ● Build Proof of concepts for quick feasibility checks. ● Own and specify the interactions between subsystems through solid understanding of the vehicle/CI architecture. ● Trade off diverse system requirements like functional , safety, reliability, durability, and compliance. ● Responsible for Interaction and Impact Analysis for any specification change and engineering change. ● Investigate system level failures because of subsystem malfunction. ● Define and solve problems from first principles. ● Gather and analyze the right data to solve problems.
