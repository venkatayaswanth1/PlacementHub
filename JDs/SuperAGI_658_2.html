Job Description

About Us: SuperAGI is pioneering the future of Artificial General Intelligence with groundbreaking research and innovative AI products. Our mission is to transform the future of applications through intelligent, autonomous solutions that drive unparalleled efficiency and growth. We are building a world where AI and human intelligence collaborate seamlessly to achieve extraordinary outcomes. If you are passionate about AI and eager to be part of a team that is shaping the future, SuperAGI is the place for you. Role Overview : As an AI Researcher at SuperAGI, you will lead and collaborate on advanced research initiatives in generative AI, AGI, and deep learning. You will work on developing novel AI models and algorithms capable of learning in a more generalized way, driving forward our mission to build intelligent agents that can adapt to a wide range of tasks and environments. If you have a passion for pushing the boundaries of AI and want to have a significant impact on the future of AGI, this is the role for you. Key Responsibilities Conduct cutting-edge research in generative AI, with a focus on AGI models, deep reinforcement learning, self-supervised learning, and multimodal systems. Design and implement novel algorithms and architectures to enable AI systems to reason, plan, and interact in complex environments. Build and optimize generative models, including transformers, GANs, VAEs, and other advanced techniques, for scalable, real-world applications. Collaborate with the engineering team to turn research ideas into scalable, production-ready solutions. Publish your research in leading conferences and journals (e.g., NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR). Stay abreast of the latest developments in AI research, bringing fresh ideas and approaches to the team. Contribute to the development of SuperAGI's broader roadmap by working closely with cross-functional teams, including product, engineering, and operations. Required Skills and Qualifications Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, or a related field (or equivalent experience). Deep understanding of generative models, reinforcement learning, deep learning architectures, and AGI principles. Strong expertise in machine learning frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or JAX. Proficiency in programming languages like Python, C++, or similar. Experience with state-of-the-art AI techniques like transformers, GANs, and variational inference. Solid background in mathematics, including probability, statistics, linear algebra, optimization, and graph theory. Proven track record of publishing research in top-tier conferences and journals. Ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. Excellent written and verbal communication skills for articulating complex ideas and research results. Preferred Qualifications Experience working on AGI or multi-modal AI systems that combine vision, language, and reasoning. Hands-on experience with scaling AI models and deploying them in production environments. Familiarity with reinforcement learning, meta-learning, or self-supervised learning. Knowledge of distributed computing and cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure). Strong problem-solving skills with a creative and innovative mindset. Experience mentoring or leading teams of researchers.
