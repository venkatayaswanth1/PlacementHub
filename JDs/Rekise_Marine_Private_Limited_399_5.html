Job Description

The Role We're seeking a passionate and driven Perception Engineer to join our team at a critical inflection point in our growth. You'll work directly with our experienced engineering team and founding members (IIT Kharagpur alumni) to develop resilient, robust autonomous marine vessels. This is a unique opportunity to work on cutting- edge AI/ML systems that enable submarines to 'see' and understand their environment using sonar, LiDAR, cameras, and multi-sensor fusion in the challenging underwater domain. As a Perception Engineer, you'll tackle some of the hardest problems in robotics — making sense of noisy acoustic signals, detecting objects in turbid water, and building robust perception systems that work reliably in environments where GPS doesn't exist and visibility is near zero. Key Responsibilities • AI/ML Pipeline Development: Develop and optimize AI/ML pipelines for sonar, LiDAR, radar, and camera-based perception tailored for marine environments. • Multi-Sensor Fusion: Design and implement frameworks that intelligently combine data from acoustic, optical, and inertial sensors for comprehensive environmental awareness. • Real-Time Object Detection: Implement object detection, segmentation, and tracking algorithms that run in real-time on embedded systems during critical underwater missions. • Robustness Enhancement: Improve perception system performance under challenging conditions including low-light environments, turbid water, noisy acoustic signals, and GPS-denied navigation. • Model Optimization: Apply quantization, pruning, knowledge distillation, and other optimization techniques to deploy sophisticated models on resource- constrained underwater systems. • End-to-End ML Development: Participate in the complete perception stack from data collection and annotation to model training, validation, and edge deployment. • Testing & Validation: Design and conduct comprehensive testing of perception systems including simulation, tank testing, and sea trials to ensure reliability. • Research & Innovation: Stay current with latest advances in computer vision and machine learning, contributing to technical papers and patents. Required Qualifications • B.Tech in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Electronics, Mathematics & Computing, or related field. • Strong foundation in machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision demonstrated through coursework, projects, or internships. • Hands-on experience with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch/TensorFlow) through academic projects or research work. • Proficient in Python and C++ with ability to write efficient, production-quality code. • Solid understanding of signal processing, linear algebra, probability, and optimization fundamentals. • Knowledge of computer vision concepts including CNNs, object detection, segmentation, and 3D vision. • Understanding of sensor fusion principles and probabilistic robotics concepts. • Demonstrated passion for robotics/AI through projects, competitions, or research publications. Preferred Experience • Research experience in computer vision, robotics, or machine learning labs. • Publications or submissions to conferences (ICRA, IROS, CVPR, ICCV, ICAR, NeurIPS) including workshop papers or preprints. • Experience with ROS/ROS2 through robotics club projects or autonomous vehicle competitions. • Projects involving SLAM, sensor fusion, 3D perception, or underwater robotics. • GPU programming experience (CUDA/OpenCL) or embedded systems optimization. • Participation in robotics competitions (Inter-IIT Tech Meet, RoboSub, IGVC or similar). • Open-source contributions to ML/robotics projects or maintained GitHub repositories.
