Job Description

Summary: We are building MFT (Medium Frequency Trading) and HFT (High Frequency Trading) systems operating at the intersection of quantitative research and high-performance technology. Our core strength lies in developing and deploying algorithmic trading strategies that operate across global markets in microseconds. We embrace a culture of collaboration, intellectual curiosity, and continuous innovation. As a Quantitative Developer, you will be immersed in the process of developing and analyzing systematic trading strategies. You’ll work with large datasets, perform statistical analysis, backtest ideas, and help enhance our trading models. This role is designed for candidates who are deeply analytical, curious about financial markets, and skilled at translating data into actionable insights. Job Responsibilities: • Alpha Research: Analyze financial and alternative data to identify patterns, signals, and anomalies that can be used to develop profitable trading strategies. • Backtesting & Simulation: Design and implement robust backtesting frameworks to test ideas using historical data, with careful attention to slippage, latency, and transaction costs. • Statistical Modeling: Apply techniques from machine learning, time series forecasting, and mathematical finance to evaluate hypotheses and build predictive models. • Data Analysis: Transform, model and analyze large datasets from multiple asset classes including equities, commodities, futures and options. • Collaboration: Work closely with traders, software engineers, and fellow quants to refine strategies and ensure seamless integration into production systems. Knowledge, Competency and Skills requirement: • Strong foundation in probability, statistics, linear algebra, optimization, and time series analysis. • Proficiency in Python, R, or MATLAB for numerical and data analysis; knowledge of C++ is a plus. • Experience working with large datasets, preferably in a Linux environment. • Familiarity with pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, PyTorch, or other relevant libraries. • Prior exposure to financial markets or algorithmic trading concepts is a plus but not required. • Strong problem-solving skills and a curiosity-driven mindset. • Passion for financial markets. • Entrepreneurial, enterprising and an agile mindset. • Ability to adapt to a dynamic work environment and show exceptional prowess at managing large impact situations. • Keen to learn, hungry for growth. • Flexibility to work independently and as a part of the team. • Strong quantitative aptitude and high EQ. Preferred Skills: • Strong Mathematics and Statistics foundation. • Experience with machine learning, reinforcement learning, or Bayesian modeling. • Participation in math/CS/quant competitions (e.g., Kaggle, QuantInsti, Olympiads). • Knowledge about financial derivatives market.
