Job Description

WHAT IT TAKES ● If you have wondered if regular Indian pottery is additively or subtractively manufactured - this is for you. ● If you think the PBF vs. DED debate is like the canon vs. nikon debate - this is for you. ● If what fascinates you about 3d printing is the organic nature of additive manufacturing - this is for you. RESPONSIBILITIES ● Analyze, build, and lead additively manufactured launch vehicle designs for all sub systems in the vehicle. ● Partner with suppliers and customers to redefine and produce scaled solutions for additive manufacturing. ● Envision required capital & infrastructure required to handle the entirety of metal printing process. ● Use proficiency in powder and materials science to provide engineering insight that leads to improvements in metal printing and metal component performance. ● Develop and apply innovative and quantitative methods for the characterization of 3D printed metal components. ● Be able to conceive designs that can only be 3d printed keeping in mind costs and mass of the product.
