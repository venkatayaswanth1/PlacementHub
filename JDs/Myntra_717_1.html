Job Description

Myntra’s Engineering team builds the technology platform that empowers our customers’ shopping experience and enables the smooth flow of products from suppliers to our customers’ doorsteps. We work on areas such as building massive-scale web-applications, engaging user-interfaces, big-data analytics, mobile apps, workflow systems, inventory management etc. We are a small technology team where each individual makes a huge impact. You will have the opportunity to be part of a rapidly growing organization and gain exposure to all the parts of a comprehensive e-commerce platform. We are seeking a highly motivated Software Engineering Intern to join our dynamic team. This internship offers hands-on experience in software development, exposure to real-world projects, and collaboration with experienced engineers. The ideal candidate has a passion for coding, problem-solving, and learning new technologies. Roles & Responsibilities ● Assist in the design, development, testing, and deployment of software applications. ● Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code. ● Participate in code reviews and contribute to team discussions. ● Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define and implement new features. ● Debug and troubleshoot software issues. ● Document technical specifications and development processes. ● Stay updated with emerging trends and technologies in software engineering.
