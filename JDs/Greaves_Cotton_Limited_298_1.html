Job Description

We are hiring a graduate engineer trainee to join Greaves for the Manufacturing and R&D profiles. The GET will be responsible for all tasks assigned by the supervisor, assisting other engineers with projects, conducting research, and writing reports. Graduate Engineer Trainee Responsibilities: •Understanding project requirements and completing all duties assigned by the Supervisor. •Shadowing senior staff members and assisting wherever possible. •Raising concerns and making suggestions for improvement where appropriate. •Conducting research and traveling to sites. •Participating in meetings and attending workshops and other training initiatives. •Analysing data and writing reports according to specifications. •Taking notes and sharing your findings with your supervisor and other relevant stakeholders. •Establishing professional relationships with staff. •Maintaining a high degree of professionalism and diligence. Graduate Engineer Trainee Requirements: •Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical / Electronics/Electrical engineering. •Strong analytical and mathematical skills •Superb time management & stakeholder management skills.
