Job Description

As a Graduate Trainee - Central Operations, you will: Run the Show: Own the heartbeat of warehouse and dark store operations—drive smooth inventory flows, replenishments, and real-time movement across the network. Master the Metrics: Track costs, resource utilisation, and performance KPIs. Strategise cost-control moves and optimise for efficiency—always keeping customer experience top priority. Predict the Peaks: Use analytics and forecasting models to anticipate demand surges, plan capacity, and allocate resources for maximum flexibility during high volume windows. Automate & Accelerate: Spot routine bottlenecks and implement tech-powered automation. Deploy digital tools and process upgrades to power speed and accuracy you can be proud of. Crack the Data Code: Dive deep into numbers—analysing inventory, cost, and manpower data to unearth trends, uncover root causes, and drive impactful improvements. Own the Collaboration: Partner with warehousing, delivery, finance, product, and category teams; solve live operational challenges and deliver seamless, scalable solutions
