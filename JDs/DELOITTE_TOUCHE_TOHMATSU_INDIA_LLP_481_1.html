Job Description

What impact will you make? Every day, your work will make an impact that matters, while you thrive in a dynamic culture of inclusion, collaboration and high performance. As the undisputed leader in professional services, Deloitte is where you’ll find unrivaled opportunities to succeed and realize your full potential. The Team What will the future of energy be? With an increased focus on decarbonisation, decentralization and digitization, the future of energy is both exciting and rapidly changing. It not only affects Energy resource and Industrials utilities but has deep cross-sectoral implications traversing the future of mobility, smart cities of the future and all user industries of the future. It is expected that most customers will want more options while digitization makes more possible, new delivery concepts may be possible in urban areas where destinations and recipients are concentrated, asset sharing may change the calculus of “ownership,” and alternative delivery models shall emerge for electricity. Ultimately, these trends will likely affect stakeholders across the ecosystem. Deloitte is an acknowledged leader of the iconic energy sector clients, for end to end transformation of their businesses in the face of industry disruption. As part of the Strategy and Operations group, Deloitte has a dedicated Energy resource and Industrials, Utilities and Resources service line focusing on providing consulting services to clients in India and the Asia Pacific. The team also collaborates with other Deloitte firms in Africa and Middle Eastern geographies as a practice recognized as a regional Centre of Excellence. The team provides advisory services in areas ranging from strategies to navigate the Future of Energy, market entry strategies, sector policies, operational transformation of entities, digitalization and enterprise technology adoption, etc. We are well integrated with our US and APAC advisory teams in brining international experience to solve our clients’ toughest challenges. • Are you a quick learner with the willingness to work on complex challenges and the changing future of energy? • Do you have an analytical mindset? • Are you concerned about climate change and prepared to lead decarbonisation of the Energy resource and Industrials sector? If yes, you will have an exciting ride with us. Work you’ll do You will be working on solving full spectrum of client challenges across a wide range of clients including public sector, private sector, Government departments, funding agencies etc. You will not just be working on ideas and recommendations to solve the client’s challenges, in most cases you would also be walking with the client in implementing those recommendations. Continuous learning at a team level and at an individual level will be your responsibility and ensuring that you have lots of fun while doing all this is our responsibility Your Prime Responsibilities • Project Delivery: Supporting specialists on projects preparing reports and conducting client meetings; understanding client requirements, project scope and deliverable requirements; following Assess-Design-Develop-Deliver-Monitor process in various work modules of the project; working across diverse teams and supporting in detailed research, analysis and ideation exercises. • Business Development: Sector research, opportunity tracking, client presentation preparation, client requirement analysis, proposal preparation, proposal presentation, business growth analysis, etc. • Practice Development: Learning and development initiatives, support in / acting as Change Catalyst to the team/project/organization. • Thought Leadership: Research, analysis and delivery of thought leadership reports (global and domestic) capturing Deloitte’s unique points of view on topical issues with guidance from senior practitioners.
