PLACEMENT Associate Analyst, Advanced Analytics Role Overview We are looking for a dynamic and motivated individual to join our team as an Associate Analyst, working at the intersection of Data Analytics, Business Strategy, and Product Management. This role is ideal for someone passionate about leveraging data and emerging technologies like Generative AI and Machine Learning to drive innovation, optimize performance, and shape product direction. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to analyze large datasets, extract actionable insights, and contribute to the development of scalable, AI-powered solutions that address real-world business challenges. Our team culture is rooted in curiosity, collaboration, and continuous learning. We encourage experimentation, value diverse perspectives, and support each other in solving complex problems. Whether you're building your first dashboard or exploring the potential of agentic AI, you'll be surrounded by passionate teammates committed to making an impact—together. 2043500 INR

Job Description

Key Responsibilities • Analyze large-scale transaction, product, and revenue datasets to uncover trends and generate actionable insights. • Support the development of AI-powered tools, dashboards, and frameworks that quantify the value of Mastercard’s services. • Translate data into compelling narratives that inform product roadmaps, feature prioritization, and go-to-market strategies. • Prototype new product ideas and solutions, especially in areas like fraud prevention, authentication, and customer experience enhancement. • Explore and apply Generative AI and Machine Learning techniques to automate insights and enhance product capabilities. • Collaborate with data engineering teams to integrate and unify new data assets. • Create visualizations and reports using tools like Tableau, Power BI, and Excel to support internal stakeholders. • Participate in brainstorming sessions, sandbox challenges, and team workshops to contribute ideas and learn continuously.
