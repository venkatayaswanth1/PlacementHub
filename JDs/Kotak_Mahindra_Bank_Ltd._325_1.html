PLACEMENT Data Scientist and Data Analyst role 1800000 INR

Job Description

Kotak Data Labs plays a pivotal role in driving innovation and business growth at Kotak. The team collaborates with stakeholders and data providers, leveraging advanced analytics and Data Science / Machine Learning techniques to drive strategic decision-making across the bank. The core work charter includes supporting different products across the bank by developing solutions across the customer journey to drive Acquisition, Engagement and Retention using cutting-edge Data Science capabilities and Data platforms. The Data Scientist and Data Analyst role within the team involves deploying projects from conception to actionable insights, enabling data-driven marketing strategies, developing risk scorecards, building behavioral models and impact in the banking business. This role focuses on leveraging Data science techniques for model development, feature engineering, driving automation, process improvement, and operational efficiency while demonstrating innovative thinking, passion for data science and fostering cross-team collaboration. Establishing strong working relationship with stakeholders in the bank is also a key aspect of this role. Roles and Responsibilities: • Deliver end to end Data Science/Analytics projects including understanding of business requirements, exploratory data analysis, data processing, aggregating data, feature engineering, building and validating predictive models, explaining model results & insights and deployment • Use AI, ML, DL techniques such as regression, classification, clustering, tree-based algorithms, support vector machine, and neural networks, recommender system, attribution modelling to address business problems. • Improve upon existing methodologies by developing new data sources/features, testing model enhancements, and fine-tuning model parameters. • Provide requirements to develop analytical capabilities, platforms, and pipelines. • Present insights and recommendations to audiences of varying levels of technical sophistication • Automate processes to ensure scalability of solutions by collaborating with cross-functional teams • Perform the data extraction, transformations, and loading from the source systems and the data lake to the analytics products using independently developed logic • Challenge the status quo with entrepreneurial innovation in curating new data sources, elevating analytics beyond data reporting and dashboarding to deliver actionable insights Skill requirements: • Master’s or Bachelor’s Degree in a quantitative discipline (Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering, etc.) • Coding skills in Python, SQL • Deep understanding and hands-on experience with ML/DL Algorithms • Experience of working with large data sets and developing scalable solutions • Knowledge of Marketing, Risk, Business, Banking and Financial services, E-Commerce is preferred • Ability to work independently and deal with ambiguity • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills • Excellent presentation and strong written & verbal communication skills • Knowledge of Banking products, Marketing strategies and Generative AI is a plus • Highly motivated and collaborative team player with strong interpersonal skills
