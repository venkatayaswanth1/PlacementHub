Job description| UI/UX Designer | NoBroker.com About Us: At NoBroker.com, we’re on a mission to make real estate simple, transparent, and broker-free. Founded in 2014 by alumni from IIT Bombay, IIT Kanpur, and IIM Ahmedabad, we’ve grown into the world’s largest brokerage-free property platform, raised $366M and backed by investors like General Atlantic, Tiger Global, and Saif Partners. We’ve built an ever-expanding ecosystem that goes far beyond property search, from NoBrokerHood for community living and Home Services for cleaning, painting, and maintenance, to NoBroker Interiors for dream home makeovers, NoBroker Payments for easy rent and maintenance transactions, and ConvoZen.ai, our AI-powered conversational intelligence platform that enables enterprises to deliver exceptional customer experiences through Agentic CX, blending Voice AI agents, AI assistance for agents, and post-interaction intelligence for QA, coaching, and insights. You will be required to take full ownership of the design project, starting from conceptualization to completion. Your contribution will add value to the team and the end product, directly impacting our customer satisfaction and profits. Job Responsibilities: Turn complex problems into clean, intuitive, and delightful experiences. Conceptualize and craft visuals, illustrations, and layouts that tell clear stories. Own features end-to-end, from concept to pixel-perfect delivery. Prototype fast, kill bad ideas faster, test, learn, and iterate fearlessly Run a creative command-center with Product, Engineering, Marketing & Content teams. Ensure consistency and usability across platforms through a strong design system mindset. Stay curious, track emerging design trends, tools, and UX best practices. Job Requirements: Bachelor's degree in design-related field 0-1 years of experience as UX/UI designer A portfolio that reflects creative problem-solving, process, and attention to detail. Proficiency with design and prototyping tools (Figma, Adobe Suite, etc) Ability to think end-to-end, from user journey to final polished product. Good command over design techniques and visual elements. Must be able to think holistically about how the UI will scale in the future Good aesthetic sense, creative thinking with attention to detail. Excellent communication, time management, and multitasking skills. Good to have skills Motion design or micro-interaction skills that add delight to experiences. Visual storytelling, brand design and illustration abilities. Sensitivity to UX writing and design language across user journeys. A mindset for testing, analytics, and understanding design impact. Strong product thinking, connecting user needs with business goals, not just designing screens Why You’ll Love Working Here Be part of a fast-growing startup where design drives product strategy Collaborate with passionate designers, PMs, and tech leaders who value craft and curiosity and work directly with founders See your designs impact millions of users across India Enjoy creative freedom, ownership, and a culture that encourages experimentation
