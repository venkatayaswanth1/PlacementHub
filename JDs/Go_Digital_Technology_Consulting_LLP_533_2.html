Job Description

About the Role We are seeking a technically strong Data Consultant who can combine data engineering fundamentals, analytical problem-solving, and consulting expertise to design, evaluate, and implement scalable data-driven solutions. The role involves working closely with clients, internal teams, and stakeholders to define strategy, evaluate systems, support technical pre-sales, and contribute to solution and product design for digital transformation initiatives. Key Responsibilities 1. Technical Consulting & Data Strategy • Conduct technical assessments of data landscapes, pipelines, architectures, and integration layers. • Develop data strategy for roadmaps covering ingestion, modeling, governance, quality, and cloud adoption. • Recommend scalable architectures aligned with client requirements (cloud, big data, ETL/ELT, MDM, data quality frameworks). 2. Data Architecture & Evaluation • Review existing systems, data sources, schemas, and pipelines to identify performance, scalability, and quality gaps. • Translate business requirements into detailed technical specifications, data models, and integration of workflows. • Evaluate tools, technologies, and platforms to support data modernization or migration initiatives. 3. Solution & Product Design • Collaborate with data engineers, architects, and product teams to design robust data solutions. • Contribute to architecture diagrams, integration patterns, API specs, data flow designs, and prototype creation. • Apply design thinking for user-centric dashboards, analytics, and data product development. 4. Technical Pre-Sales & Internal Enablement • Prepare and deliver technical solution decks, architecture proposals, and capability presentations. • Support RFP/RFI responses with solution design, effort estimation, and technical validation. • Work with internal sales teams to create demos, POVs, and reusable assets for clients. 5. Stakeholder Collaboration & Project Enablement • Facilitate requirement workshops, technical discussions, and architecture reviews with client teams. • Act as a bridge between business users, engineering teams, and leadership to ensure alignment. • Drive clarity in scope, technical deliverables, risks, and dependencies. 6. Documentation & Technical Communication • Create technical documents including data assessment reports, solution architecture documents, HLDs/LLDs, evaluation matrices, and POV summaries. • Present complex technical concepts in a structured and simplified manner for business stakeholders. Technical Skills (Preferred) • Understanding data engineering fundamentals (ETL/ELT, data pipelines, orchestration). • Good grasp of SQL, Python, APIs, cloud services (AWS/Azure/GCP), and modern data stack tools. • Knowledge of data modeling (dimensional, relational), metadata management, and data quality frameworks. • Awareness of visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau) and prototyping tools (Figma, Miro). • Exposure to architecture patterns such as microservices, event-driven systems, modern data platforms, and lakehouse paradigms
