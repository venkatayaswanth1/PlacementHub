Data Analytics & Reporting  Develop, maintain, and automate MIS dashboards related to:  Extract and analyze data from CBS, LOS/LMS, Trade Finance, and other systems to identify process gaps, exceptions, and control breaches.  Support preparation of management and regulatory reports (e.g., RBI, Internal Audit, HO reviews).  Design data models for monitoring key performance indicators (KPIs) and service level adherence.
