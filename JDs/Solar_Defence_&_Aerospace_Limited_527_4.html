Job Description

Description "Skilled Systems Engineer with a background in Aviation related communications both LOS and BLOS. Testing, evaluation, and integration of systems, coordination with vendors, and documentation. Familiar with transceivers, power amplifiers, and PCB-level hardware, and should be comfortable working in a lab environment with test equipment. Candidates with hands-on development and programming experience in relevant areas will be given preference." Responsibilities "Develop and prototype innovative new technologies to implement software defined radio systems, antennas, and circuits, for various applications, ensuring optimal performance and reliability. Develop, test and troubleshoot SDR hardware, FPGA development systems, and RF signals using test and measurement equipment. Support multiple programs developing and testing novel processing algorithms running on a software defined radio. Evaluate integrated systems for performance and functionality in communication scenarios. Develop software and firmware source code in C/C++, Embedded C, and other languages to implement, and maintain test harnesses used to support wireless communications product and prototype development. Develop and plan for SDR operational testing to demonstrate performance in communication and networking systems. Optimize and refine SDR algorithms and implementations to meet evolving operational needs. Prepare technical documentation, including design specifications, test reports and manuals."
