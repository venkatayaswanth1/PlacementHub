Job Description

Role Overview Join Tanla’s cutting-edge Data Science Team, where innovation meets scale. You’ll work with massive datasets across messaging, telecom, and enterprise communication, building AI-powered models that impact millions of users in real time. If you’re passionate about Machine Learning, AI, NLP, and Big Data, this is your opportunity to build and deploy production-scale intelligence systems from Day 1. As a Data Science Engineer at Tanla, you will: • Explore and analyze data: Collect, clean, and visualize large-scale datasets to uncover trends, patterns, and actionable insights. • Engineer features: Design meaningful data transformations and feature sets that enhance model accuracy and robustness. • Build predictive models: Develop and experiment with ML models for prediction, optimization, and self-learning applications. • Work on NLP & LLM projects: Build and fine-tune text intelligence models for intent detection, message routing, summarization, and generative AI use cases. • Evaluate and fine-tune models: Assess model performance using key metrics, and fine-tune them to customize AI solutions for specific business use cases and real-world scenarios. • Deploy and monitor models: Take models to real-world production using scalable APIs, automation workflows, and real-time data pipelines. • Collaborate cross-functionally: Work closely with product, engineering, and business teams to translate data-driven insights into impactful decisions and features. Who Can Apply • Strong fundamentals in Statistics, Probability, Linear Algebra, and Machine Learning. • Proficiency in Python and exposure to data handling (NumPy, Pandas, SQL). • Familiarity with ML algorithms, model evaluation, and exploratory data analysis. • Passion for solving real-world problems using AI and innovation. Preferred (Good to Have) • Projects in AI / ML / NLP / GenAI / LLMs (academic or personal). • Understanding of the telecom or enterprise communication domain (optional) is a plus. Why join us? • Impactful Work: Solve meaningful real-life business problems by building cutting-edge products. • Tremendous Growth Opportunities: Work in a fast-growing CPaaS and product-driven culture with scope for continuous professional development. • Innovative Environment: Be part of a world-class team that loves solving tough problems and values innovation. Tanla is an equal opportunity employer. We champion diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees. 🌐 www.tanla.com
