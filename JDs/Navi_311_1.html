Job Description

SDE I Location: Bangalore About the Team Navi’s Engineering team builds the backbone of our financial products—spanning lending, payments, insurance, investments, and debt management. We operate as cross-functional teams that work closely with Product, Data, and Business functions to deliver reliable, high-performance systems at scale. Our engineers focus on solving real-world challenges through scalable architecture, automation, and long-term thinking—ensuring every Navi product is built to serve millions efficiently and seamlessly. About the Role As an SDE-1 at Navi, you will be building large-scale distributed systems that power our core financial products across lending, insurance, UPI, and investments. The role requires strong computer science fundamentals and the ability to design solutions that are scalable, secure, and reliable. At Navi, engineers take complete ownership—from understanding requirements and designing systems to coding, deploying, and monitoring in production. You’ll work closely with senior engineers and architects, learning best practices while also being trusted with real impact from Day 1. This role gives you the opportunity to solve challenging engineering problems, contribute directly to products used by millions, and accelerate your growth in a fast-paced, engineering-first culture. What We Expect From You Write clean, efficient, and testable code in at least one programming language. Contribute to designing scalable systems and services. Debug, optimize, and improve performance of existing applications. Collaborate with peers and product teams to deliver business-critical features. Take ownership of assigned modules and deliver with accountability. Continuously learn and adapt to new tools, frameworks, and best practices. Must Haves Strong foundation in Data Structures, Algorithms, and Object-Oriented Design. Proficiency in at least one programming language such as Java, Go, Kotlin, Python, JavaScript/TypeScript. Solid understanding of databases, operating systems, and basic networking concepts. Collaborative attitude and willingness to learn fast in a dynamic environment. Strong debugging and problem-solving skills with attention to detail. Ability to work through ambiguity and build solutions in a fast-paced environment. Collaborative mindset, eagerness to learn quickly, and willingness to take ownership. From Personal & Home Loans to UPI, Insurance, Mutual Funds, and Gold — we’re building tech-first solutions that work at scale, with a strong customer-first approach. If you’re driven to deliver real value to customers, no matter the challenge, this is the place for you. We chase excellence by uplifting each other—and that starts with every one of us. Why You'll Thrive at Navi At Navi, it’s about how you think, build, and grow. You’ll thrive here if: You’re impact-driven You take ownership, build boldly, and care about making a real difference. You strive for excellence Good isn’t good enough. You bring focus, precision, and a passion for quality. You embrace change You adapt quickly, move fast, and always put the customer first.
