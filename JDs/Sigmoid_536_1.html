Job Description

About the Role: We are seeking a Data Scientist to work on a diverse range of cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) problems across multiple industries. The role offers an opportunity to tackle complex, structured business challenges using ML, Deep Learning (DL), and Generative AI technologies. If you are passionate about applying advanced analytics to build impactful tools and products, we would love to connect with you. Key Responsibilities: • Enhance the accuracy, scalability, and effectiveness of models deployed in production across ML, DL, and Generative AI domains. • Optimise data workflows, including data ingestion, profiling, exploratory data analysis (EDA), cleaning, transformation, quality checks, feature engineering, and thorough documentation. • Develop clean, well-structured, and well-documented code that adheres to team standards and ensures reproducibility. • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to build, deploy, and productionize models and workflows that address real-world AI/ML/DL use cases. Key Skills and Expertise: • Strong learning agility with a structured and analytical mindset. • Solid understanding of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Generative AI concepts. • Proficiency in Python for data wrangling, analysis, visualization, and modeling. • Hands-on experience with Python libraries, Gen AI frameworks, Git, and Streamlit. • Exposure to distributed data processing tools such as PySpark and Jupyter. • Experience deploying at least one industrial ML project (supervised or unsupervised). • Strong data interpretation and problem-solving skills. • Clear and confident communicator, able to articulate complex technical concepts simply. • Collaborative team player with a high sense of ownership and accountability. • Ability to perform effectively in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment. • Passion for technology, innovation, and continuous learning.
