(Kenzen Ventures Private Limited) JD - Founders Office Growth About Earthful Earthful is a VC funded, FMCG company focused in the health and wellness space. We recently featured on Shark Tank India, and are on a mission to build India’s most trusted clean-label wellness brand. The founders (Sudha Gogineni and Veda Gogineni) are graduates from IIT Kharagpur and ISB with a multitude of experience across multinational companies & startups, such as Uber, Meesho, Deutsche Bank, ITC. Earthful is an early stage startup and hence the candidate will have the opportunity to work closely with the founders. We are looking for a candidate passionate about scaling a startup. About Role Join the Founder’s Office to work directly with the founders of Earthful, driving key strategic projects, business decisions, and operational initiatives. You’ll collaborate with cross-functional teams, conduct market research, analyze business performance, and manage special projects like product launches and new market strategies. Location: Full-time Mumbai Responsibilities ● Collaborate with the founders to develop and implement long-term strategies. ● Conduct competitive analysis and market research to guide decision-making. ● Work with teams across the business to execute strategic projects. ● Analyze business data and provide actionable insights for growth. ● Lead special projects like new market entries, product launches, and process optimizations. ● Help prepare materials for investor communications and funding rounds. ● Assist with daily operations and key business decisions. Skill Sets ● Bachelor’s/Master’s degree ● Strong analytical skills with a data-driven approach. ● Excellent communication and stakeholder management abilities. ● Entrepreneurial mindset with a high degree of ownership. ● Proven project management and problem-solving skills.
