Job Description

About Newton School:- Come be part of a rocketship that’s creating a massive impact on the world of technology! On one side you have over a million college graduates every year with barely 5% employability rates and on the other side, there are 1000s of companies struggling to find talent. Newton School aims to bridge this massive gap through its personalized learning platform. We are building an online university and solving the deep problem of employability of graduates. We have a strong core team consisting of alumni from IIT's and IIM’s, having several years of industry experience in companies like Unacademy, Inmobi among others. On this mission, we are backed by some of the most respected investors around the world, - Nexus Venture Partners, RTP Global, Steadview Capital and a slew of angel investors including CRED’s Kunal Shah, Flipkart’s Kalyan Krishnamoorthy, Unacademy founders, Udaan’s Sujeet Kumar among others. About The Role:- We are looking for a dynamic Associate Program Manager to join our team. This role will focus on identifying and solving day-to-day challenges in the learning domain, with a particular emphasis on curriculum, content, and student progression. The ideal candidate will excel at uncovering bottlenecks, implementing immediate solutions, and setting up scalable, long-term processes. This role is pivotal in ensuring the smooth functioning of our academic operations and delivering exceptional learning experiences. Responsibilities:- • Identify and analyze day-to-day operational challenges in areas such as curriculum design, content development, and student progression. • Develop and implement solutions to resolve immediate issues and enhance overall efficiency. • Work closely with academic teams to uncover bottlenecks and create streamlined workflows. • Set up scalable processes to support the learning ecosystem as the organization grows. • Collaborate with multiple stakeholders, including academic teams, content creators, and leadership, to align on goals and ensure seamless execution. • Track the effectiveness of implemented solutions and refine them based on feedback and data-driven insights.
