Job Description

What impact will you make? Every day at Deloitte, your contribution will make an impact that matters while you thrive in a culture of inclusion, collaboration and high performance. As the undisputed leader in professional services, Deloitte in India is positioned to become the largest advisory firm in India. At Deloitte, you will find opportunities that are unmatched to realize your full potential and succeed. The Team - Economic Development and Urban /Government Transformation (EDUT) In today’s world economic development and enhancing liveability requires coordinated efforts from Government, Private Sector and Citizens. This requires formulating effective strategies, policies and improving regulatory environment. • Deloitte EDUT practice is assisting multiple clients including donor/bilateral organisations, Federal, State and City Governments, development agencies as well as private sector organisations on the following aspects • Design and implement large government transformation initiatives – impacting citizen, businesses and society; ensuring sustainability & inclusion • Formulate strategies, policies and guidelines for fostering economic development and improving quality of life • Leverage ICT for improving government service delivery and promoting investments Deloitte’s EDUT consulting practice is consistently ranked #1 or #2 in the industry, achieving regular double-digit growth in targeted capability areas. The practice comprises practitioners from diverse areas such as finance, strategy, analytics and digital, planning, social and economic development, and business restructuring. Deloitte invests in outstanding people of diverse talents and backgrounds and empowers them. And we are looking for people who • are keen to make an impactful impact to the society and environment. • are quick learners with the willingness to work on complex challenges and the changing future of cities, economic development, urban and government transformation. • Enjoy leveraging research, analytics and industry insights to create value for clients. • Have an analytical mindset. Prime Responsibilities The primary role of an Analyst is to make immediate, direct contributions to enhancing our clients’ competitive position and performance in ways that are distinctive, innovative, and sustainable. You will work in teams led by partners with colleagues and clientele from a wide variety of industries to develop innovative operating solutions by bringing in a broad perspective. As part of these engagements, you will develop a deep understanding of client issues, work in teams to build robust and executable solutions and articulate effectively to the client stakeholders. In cross-border engagements, you will also have the opportunity to work with our practitioners from our practices from across the world. These provide a strong platform for you to expand your own knowledge and skills while helping our clients to achieve competitive success You are expected to work in a fast paced environment while we provide personalized training programs to prepare you for the role in attractive conditions and bright career prospects aligned to your experience.
