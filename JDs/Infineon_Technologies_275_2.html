Job Description

Your Career Starts Here! Are you a passionate problem-solver with a strong foundation in system engineering? Do you thrive on a challenge and want to make a real impact on how complex systems are designed and built? We're looking for a Junior Systems & Performance Engineer to join our dynamic team and help us build the future of technology. What You'll Do You won't just be an observer—you'll be a key player from day one. You'll dive deep into: • Building the Blueprint: You'll use tools like System Composer to architect and model system-level designs, essentially creating the digital blueprint of our systems. • Bringing It to Life: You'll develop system virtual prototypes and performance models, bringing those blueprints to life and analysing how they perform under pressure. • The Power of Code: You'll use your expertise in C++, Python, SystemC, and System Verilog to implement, test, and debug these models. • Component-Level Deep Dive: You'll get hands-on with SPICE modeling to create detailed electronic component models. • Finding the Fix: You'll work side-by-side with experienced engineers to identify performance bottlenecks and propose innovative solutions. Who We're Looking For You're a recent graduate or early-career professional with a degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering (or a related field). You have a solid grasp of system engineering fundamentals and are eager to learn and grow. Most importantly, you are excited about the chance to apply your skills in C++, Python, SystemC, and System Verilog to real-world challenges.
