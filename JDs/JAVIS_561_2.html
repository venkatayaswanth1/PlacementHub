Job Description

JAVIS is looking for a Data Engineer who will design, build, and optimize scalable data pipelines and infrastructure that power next-generation analytics and AI systems. You will work on real-time data extraction, transformation, and integration projects that directly impact how enterprises use data to make faster, better decisions. Responsibilities: ● Design and develop data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) pipelines using Python, AWS, and other big data frameworks ● Deploy data models, ML workflows, and analytics pipelines to transform raw data into actionable business intelligence ● Collaborate with product, AI, and engineering teams to build data-driven features for enterprise customers ● Optimize data architecture, storage, and query performance for scalability and cost efficiency ● Analyse and visualize large datasets to identify patterns, trends, and anomalies ● Build tools to monitor, validate, and ensure data integrity and quality Requirements: ● B.Tech / B.S. / Dual degree in CSE, M&C, EE or equivalent ● Strong academic record with a minimum CGPA of 8 & above is a must ● Proficiency in Python and its libraries such as Pandas, PySpark, or PyArrow ● Strong foundation in Data Structures, Algorithms, OOPs, and DBMS ● Understanding of SQL, ETL processes, and cloud platforms (preferably AWS) ● Exposure to statistical analysis, data modelling, and machine learning concepts ● Familiarity with distributed data systems such as Apache Hudi, Spark or Kafka is a plus ● Excellent problem-solving and analytical abilities ● Strong communication skills with the ability to work collaboratively in fast-paced teams
