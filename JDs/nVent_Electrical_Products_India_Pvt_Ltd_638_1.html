Job Description

1. ERP Technical Track Candidates who pursue rotations within the ERP teams will gain exposure to several ERPs at nVent over the course of three years in the DLDP, learning skills that will prepare them for a full-time ERP role. Potential rotation opportunities within the ERP track include: • Infor M3 • Syteline • Oracle • Epicor • SAP In these roles you will be part of a global and multinational team with ERP analysts located in North America, Europe, India and China. Some skills you will learn could include troubleshooting system problems, provisioning and monitoring user access, analyzing secure data to maintain system integrity and learn deep knowledge in ERP fundamentals, different database knowledge, system programming and cloud concepts. 2. DGTM Track Candidates who pursue rotation within the DGTM teams will gain exposure to Sales and Marketing applications like Salesforce, Acquia CMS, Marketo, Informatica P360 and many other applications. In this role, you could start working as a Business System Analyst working with stakeholders to develop requirements, document system and data diagrams and help with testing for production releases. Subsequent years in the rotation, you could learning development in Salesforce, on our core websites or our product information system. Dependent on skillset and interest, candidate may work on self-service tools like CPQ, order lookup and customer care
