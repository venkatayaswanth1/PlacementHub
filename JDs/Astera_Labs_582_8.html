Job Description

**Physical Design (PD):** Engage in physical design activities such as floorplanning, placement, routing, clock tree synthesis (CTS), static timing analysis (STA), and physical verification. Perform power and reliability checks to ensure design quality and manufacturability. This position provides an excellent opportunity to develop a strong foundation in SoC development, working alongside industry experts on cutting-edge connectivity technologies that power tomorrow’s data centers.
