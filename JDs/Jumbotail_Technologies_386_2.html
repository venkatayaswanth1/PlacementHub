The Jumbotail Graduate Leader Program (GLP) is Jumbotail’s flagship initiative to develop the next generation of business leaders. The program offers graduates a structured, three-level growth path that combines on-ground experience, learning interventions, and leadership mentoring. In Year 1, GLP Fellows work in key business functions such as retail operations, supply chain, category management, decision science, technology or business enablement, building a deep understanding of Jumbotail’s ecosystem. Based on performance and leadership potential, Fellows progress to GLP Level 2, where they lead teams, drive critical projects, and deliver measurable business impact. Through continuous coaching from senior leaders, capability-building sessions, and real-world problem-solving opportunities, the program accelerates leadership readiness. On successful completion, Fellows transition into managerial or specialist roles such as Product Manager, Program Manager, Decision Scientist, Category Manager, and more that contribute directly to Jumbotail’s mission of building for India''''s retail ecosystem.
