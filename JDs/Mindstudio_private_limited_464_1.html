Job Description

About the Role: We are looking for innovative and tech-driven architecture graduates who are passionate about integrating design thinking with digital technology. As a Digital Workflow Architect, you will play a key role in creating platforms, dashboards, and tools that simplify how data from Revit and BIM is communicated to designers, consultants, and clients. You will work in a startup-style environment, where curiosity, experimentation, and ownership are valued as much as technical skills. This role offers the freedom to learn, ideate, and implement solutions that redefine how design and quantity workflows function in a digital ecosystem. Key Responsibilities: • Understand project data and quantities extracted from Revit and assist in developing visual dashboards for BOQ and QS analysis. • Collaborate with Designers to develop intuitive user interfaces that make complex data easy to understand for non-Revit users. • Developing digital workflow platforms that connect clients, consultants, and in-house teams seamlessly. • Research and propose workflow automation ideas to enhance collaboration across disciplines. • Coordinate with architecture and QS teams to identify process challenges and suggest digital improvements. ________________________________________ Required Skills & Competencies: • Strong understanding of architectural workflows and project documentation. • Basic familiarity with Revit / BIM processes (quantities, schedules, parameters, etc.). • Familiarity with digital tools and coding fundamentals (Python / JavaScript/ APIs). • Ability to visualize data and develop conceptual dashboards or workflow diagrams. • Analytical mindset with a creative and problem-solving approach. • Good communication and coordination skills. • Eagerness to learn new digital tools. ________________________________________ Core Values We Look For: • Innovation: You love to explore how design and data can connect. • Ownership: You take responsibility for outcomes, not just tasks. • Collaboration: You value teamwork across design and tech. • Curiosity: You’re eager to learn how technology shapes the built environment. • Adaptability: You thrive in an open, dynamic, and startup-like workspace. • Integrity: You uphold honesty and accountability in every task.
