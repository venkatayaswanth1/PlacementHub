Insurity’s Next Software Engineer: We are looking for highly motivated engineers with strong full stack development and AI/ML foundations to join our Innovation & Product Engineering teams. The ideal candidate will design, develop, and deploy intelligent enterprise-grade applications leveraging modern frameworks, scalable architecture, and machine learning models. What Our Software Engineer Will Do: • Develop and maintain scalable full stack applications using modern front-end and back-end technologies. • Design and implement ML-driven features (recommendation systems, NLP-based insights, anomaly detection, etc.) into core insurance products. • Collaborate with product managers, data scientists, and DevOps teams to deliver robust, end-to-end digital solutions. • Build APIs and microservices for data processing, analytics, and seamless system integration. • Optimize performance, maintain clean architecture, and ensure code quality across deployments. • Participate in system design discussions, code reviews, and continuous integration processes. • Research and experiment with new technologies, frameworks, and AI techniques to drive innovation. Who We’re Looking For: • Strong programming skills in Python, Java, or Node.js and similar programming languages • Hands-on experience with front-end frameworks like React.js, Angular, or Vue.js. • Deep understanding of data structures, algorithms, and software design principles. • Experience with ML frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or scikit-learn. • Familiarity with RESTful APIs, microservices architecture, and cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP). • Knowledge of databases (SQL and NoSQL). • Solid understanding of version control (Git), containerization (Docker), and CI/CD setups. • Excellent problem-solving, analytical, and teamwork abilities. Thank you for your interest in Insurity!
