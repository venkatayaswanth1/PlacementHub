Job Description

As a Junior Product Designer at DoubleTick — the flagship enterprise product by Apport Software Solutions Pvt. Ltd. — you will be at the heart of designing user interfaces for a powerful WhatsApp-based Customer Relations and Communication platform used by over 7,000 businesses across 140+ countries. DoubleTick helps sales teams streamline lead management, collaborate more effectively, and drive results — all within the Meta ecosystem. In this role, you’ll be creating low-fidelity mockups, high-fidelity mockups, and conducting usability testing to ensure your designs are both visually appealing and functional while working closely with senior designers, product managers, and engineers to translate briefs into multiple design options, iterate quickly, and ensure a smooth handoff to development. Beyond the product team, you’ll work cross-functionally with customer support, customer success, and marketing teams to ensure the design aligns with both user needs and business goals
